23 Business review Business review RBS is now structured to deliver its ambition by organising itself around the needs of its customers, so as to combine customer groups with similar needs into franchises able to deliver co-ordinated services.
The reorganised bank will be a UK-focused retail and corporate bank with an international footprint to drive its corporate business.
The previously reported operating divisions are now realigned into three franchises: Personal & Business Banking Commercial & Private Banking Corporate & Institutional Banking In addition, RBS will continue to manage and report Citizens Financial Group and RBS Capital Resolution separately until disposal or wind down.
