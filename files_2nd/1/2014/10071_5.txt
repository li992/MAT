Strategy Christopher Miller Since Melroses flotation in 2003, and the Chairman introduction of our buy, improve, sell 4March 2015 strategy, we have seen many changes in economic and stock market conditions.
Nevertheless, through all this, returns to shareholders have been extremely good and we see no reason to diverge from our proven strategy.
We continue to see situations which are candidates for our management and investment methods and we are confident we will identify a suitable opportunity in due course.
In the meantime, we will continue to improve our existing businesses, and will make disposals at the appropriate time.
