STRATEGY Our long-term strategy remains the same as that set at our Listing in 2005 to optimise the performance of our assets, deliver our growth projects and take advantage of natural resource opportunities in Central Asia.
In 2009, we focused on the first part of our strategy with considerable success, as seen in these results.
In 2010, there is still much work to be done on optimising and improving our efficiency, and we are in a good position to deliver on the second part of our strategy, and move our growth projects forward.
