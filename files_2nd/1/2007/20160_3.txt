3i Group plc 03 Report and accounts 2007 3i at a glance 3i Group is a private equity and venture capital business making and managing investments in Europe, Asia and the US.
The Group is a FTSE 100 company, providing shareholders with liquid access to private equity returns through five types of investment activity, Buyouts, Growth Capital, Venture Capital, Infrastructure and QPE private equity for quoted companies.
Investments are made using funds from the Groups own balance sheet and from funds which the Group manages or advises for others.
3i covers a range of private equity and venture capital activity providing capital for businesses at all stages of their development.
