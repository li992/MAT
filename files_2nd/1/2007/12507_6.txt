06 Premier Foods plc Annual report and accounts 2007 Chairmans statement 2007 has been a year of integration and change for Premier Foods.
A year when we completed the integration of Campbells and the acquisition of RHM to create the UKs largest food producer.
A year when we laid down the foundations for a stronger business in the future in terms of operations, supply chain, and IT systems and a year when the business was impacted by unprecedented cost inflation.
