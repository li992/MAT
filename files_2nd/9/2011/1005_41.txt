We conducted our audits of the Groups financial statements for each of the two years ended December 31, 2011 in accordance with ISAs.
If we had been required to conduct the audits of the Groups financial statements for each of the two years ended December 31, 2011 in accordance with Canadian GAAS there would have been no material differences in the form or content of our audit reports.
Furthermore an auditors report prepared in accordance with reporting standards under Canadian GAAS on the aforementioned consolidated financial statements would not contain a qualification.
