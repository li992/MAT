Directors pensions The following information relates to the pension arrangements provided for Mike Bramley, Tim Clarke, Tony Hughes and Karim Naffah under the Dened benefit Section of the Mitchells & Butlers Executive Pension Plan the Plan, and in the cases of Tim Clarke, Tony Hughes and Karim Naffah under the unfunded Executive Top-Up Scheme MABETUS.
The Plan is a funded, Inland Revenue approved, occupational pension scheme with a final salary section of which these Directors are members.
All Plan benets are subject to Inland Revenue limits.
Where such limitation is due to the earnings cap, MABETUS is used to provide pension and death benets to the level that would otherwise have applied.
The Plans main features applicable to the Executive Directors are: A normal pension age of 60: Life assurance cover of four times pensionable salary: Pension accrual of 1 30th of final pensionable Pensions payable in the event of ill health: and salary for each year of pensionable service: Spouses and dependants pensions on death.
