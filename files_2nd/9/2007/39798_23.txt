42 v Cash settled overseas senior employees scheme The plan for overseas senior employees is a cash settlement scheme which mirrors the performance of the Executive share option plan in 42 i above.
A notional number of shares are granted to the employee equal to 100 per cent of their salary and divided by the share price on the date of the grant.
78,271 shares were granted in 2005, based on the performance and assumptions of the Executive share option plan on 29 April 2005.
The Black-Scholes model was used to fair value these shares at prevailing market rates, at a share price of 476.8p per share.
