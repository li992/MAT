Business review Governance Accounts Remuneration report The Capita Group Plc 86 Satisfaction of options When satisfying awards made under its share plans and long term incentive plans, the Company uses newly issued, treasury or purchased shares as appropriate.
Dilution All awards are made under plans that incorporate dilution limits as set out in the Guidelines for Share Incentive Schemes published by the Association of British Insurers.
The current estimated dilution from existing awards including executive and all-employee share awards is approximately 7.2% of the Companys share capital as at 31 December 2010.
