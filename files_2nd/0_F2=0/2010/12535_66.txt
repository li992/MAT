Capital management The primary objective of the groups capital management is to ensure that it maintains healthy capital ratios in order to support its business and increase shareholder value.
The group manages its capital structure and makes adjustments to it in light of changes in economic conditions.
No changes were made in the objectives, policies or processes during the years ended 31 December 2010 and 31 December 2009.
The group monitors capital using a gearing ratio, which is net debt divided by net assets.
The groups policy is to keep the long-term gearing ratio below 50 per cent.
Net debt comprises interest-bearing bank loans and convertible bonds, less cash and short-term deposits.
