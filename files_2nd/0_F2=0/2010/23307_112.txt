Explanatory Notes to Resolution 12 Notice of period for general meetings This resolution is required as the implementation of the EU Shareholder Rights Directive has increased the notice period for general meetings of companies to 21 days unless certain conditions are met in which case it may be 14 days.
To ensure that the Companys general meetings other than AGMs may be held on 14 days notice, a shareholder resolution reducing the period of notice to not less than 14 days must have been passed at the immediately preceding AGM.
