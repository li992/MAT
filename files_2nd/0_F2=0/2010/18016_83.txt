External Non-executive Directorships The Companys policy is to allow Executive Directors to retain fees received for serving as a Nonexecutive Director of a company outside the Capco group.
During the period Ian Hawksworth received a fee of 20,000 in respect of his Non-executive Directorship of AIM-listed Japan Residential Investment Company Limited.
No other Executive Director currently serves as a Non-executive Director elsewhere.
