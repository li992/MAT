Total shareholder return Total shareholder return TSR is the measure of the returns that a company has generated for its shareholders, reecting share price movements and assuming reinvestment of dividends.
BTs TSR for the 2010 financial year was positive 62.4%, compared with the FTSE 100 TSR which was positive 50.4% and the FTSEurorst 300 Telco Index TSR which was positive 27.9%.
BTs TSR improvement in the 2010 financial year is mainly due to the recovery of the share price during 2010, from a closing price of 71.4p in March 2009.
