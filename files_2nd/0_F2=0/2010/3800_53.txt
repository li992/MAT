344,655 shares are held by the Compass Group Employee Share Trust ESOP and 17,209 shares by the Compass Group Long Term Incentive Plan Trust LTIPT.
These shares are listed on a recognised stock exchange and their market value at 30 September 2010 was 1.9 million 2009: 2.6 million.
ESOP and LTIPT are discretionary trusts for the benefit of employees and the shares held are used to satisfy some of the Groups liabilities to employees for share options, share bonus and long-term incentive plans.
All of the shares held by the ESOP and LTIPT are required to be made available in this way.
The merger reserve arose in 2000 following the demerger from Granada Compass plc.
The equity adjustment for put options arose in 2005 on the accounting for the options held by the Groups minority partners requiring the Group to purchase those minority interests.
