Financial statements 121 40 Pensions a Current pension arrangements Earls Court & Olympia group EC&O has a hybrid pension scheme comprising an ongoing money purchase section and a final salary section which was closed to new members in 2000, but continues to accrue future service benets for those employees who were members at date of closure.
The final salary section is a funded dened benefit scheme which is contracted out of State Second Pension.
The Groups current policy is largely to provide future retirement benets through dened contribution arrangements.
