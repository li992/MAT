SHARE-BASED PAYMENT In 2009 1,255,205 ordinary shares awards with a par value of $0.0000125 each were awarded to Directors and senior management as part of the IPO plan.
The share awards are conditional on the employee completing three years service the vesting period from the date of the IPO, during which any dealings are prohibited.
These share awards are not subjected to any performance conditions.
The Group has no legal or constructed obligation to repurchase the shares related to the granted share awards.
During the year ended 31 December 2010 747,349 ordinary share awards were forfeited due to the termination of the employment with the Company by five senior managers, of which 302,880 share awards were granted to the new senior managers.
These share awards are also conditional on the completion of three years service the vesting period with any dealings prohibited during that period.
