Governance Remuneration Report continued Directors Pension Benefits Mr F welham is a deferred member of the Final Salary Defined Benefit section of the Shanks Group Pension Scheme, which is a funded scheme, approved by HMRC.
Pension benefits are capped at the notional HMRC earnings cap, which is reviewed annually.
For the year to 31 March 2010 the notional cap was 123,600.
Under the terms of this scheme participating members have: at retirement, and subject to length of service, a pension of up to two thirds of basic salary: an employee contribution of 7% of basic salary: a lump sum death in service benefit of four times basic salary: and a spouses pension on death.
