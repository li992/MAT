Borrowings In February 2010, the Company renegotiated its loan facility with The Royal Bank of Scotland plc, The Bank of Scotland plc and Unicredit Bank AG.
Under the terms of the renegotiations, the term of the loan facility was extended from March 2011 until January 2013 and the size of the facility was reduced from 550 million to 325 million, with further step-downs to 320 million in March 2011, 315 million in September 2011 and 250 million in March 2012.
The facility is subject to financial covenants see note 20.
The Company made net repayments totalling 87.5 million from the facility during the year and had total outstanding drawings of 22.5 million 19.3 million at 31 December 2010.
