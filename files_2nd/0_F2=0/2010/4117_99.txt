Share-based payments reserve this reserve records the fair value of share-based payments expensed in the income statement.
Hedging reserve this reserve records the portion of the gain or loss on a hedging instrument in a cash flow hedge that is determined to be an effective hedge.
Translation reserve this reserve records exchange differences arising from the translation of the financial statements of foreign subsidiaries.
