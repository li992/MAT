The majority of the Groups trade receivables relate to customer payments awaiting collection.
Since the timing of collection is controlled by the Group and is within a few days of processing the transaction, credit risk is considered to be not applicable to these items.
Where credit is offered to customers, the average credit period offered is 41 days 2009: 39 days.
No interest is charged on trade receivables at any time.
Disclosures regarding credit risk below relate only to customers offered credit.
Individually or collectively material trade receivables are reviewed for recoverability when an adverse change in credit quality is identified or when they become overdue.
Credit risk is limited as exposure is spread over a large number of counterparties and customers.
