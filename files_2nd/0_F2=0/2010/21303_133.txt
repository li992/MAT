The Company was not exposed at 31st December 2010 or 2009 to significant risks arising from the financial instruments it holds, which comprised cash, balances with central banks and other assets which had no credit or market risk.
During 2008 Barclays Bank PLC issued 4,050m of Mandatorily Convertible Notes MCNs, which had mandatorily converted into ordinary shares of Barclays PLC by 30th June 2009.
Barclays PLCs right to receive the MCNs was included in other assets in 2008, with a corresponding increase, net of issue costs, in other equity.
In 2009, Barclays PLC waived its rights over the MCNs, which were added to its cost of investment in its subsidiary.
