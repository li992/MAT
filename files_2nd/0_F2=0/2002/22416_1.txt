The City of London Investment Trust plc has a portfolio predominantly invested in larger companies.
It has a wholly independent board of directors and is managed by Henderson Global Investors Limited.
Its prime objective is to outperform over the long term the total return of the FTSE All-Share Index through either capital growth or income generation.
The board will continue to recognise the importance of income to shareholders.
