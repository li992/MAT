Shareholder information and financial calendar Website Information about Taylor Nelson Sofres, including share price information, is available on the groups internet site www.
Material likely to be of particular interest to investors can be found in the Investor Centre.
The site contains copies of announcements made to the London Stock Exchange, interactive versions of the groups annual reports, as well as webcasts of the companys financial results presentations.
It is possible to register on the site to receive email alerts when new announcements become available on the internet.
The TNS Investor Centre won the 2002 Investor Relations Best Practice Website Award for a FTSE 250 company.
Registrar The companys registrar is Computershare Investor Services PLC.
Any enquiries about shareholdings in Taylor Nelson Sofres plc, for example changes to name or address or loss of share certificate, should be sent in writing to: Computershare Investor Services PLC, PO Box 82, The Pavilions, Bridgwater Road, Bristol BS99 7NH.
Please ensure that all correspondence refers to Taylor Nelson Sofres plc and clearly states the registered name and address of the shareholder.
Electronic communications Shareholders are able to ask the company to communicate with them electronically, rather than by post.
Shareholders wishing to take advantage of this service may do so by registering their email address on the Computershare website, which can be accessed through the Investor Centre at www.
Registration is free and easy to complete.
All that is required for registration is the shareholder reference number that is shown on dividend tax vouchers and share certificates.
Shareholders who have registered for this service will be sent an electronic notification to let them know when the companys annual report becomes available on the groups website.
Shareholders are able to look up a range of information on the Computershare website, including the number of Taylor Nelson Sofres shares they hold, details of the registered name and address and dividend payment instructions.
If shareholders have more than one account arising from inconsistencies in name and address details, they may avoid duplicate sets of mailings by asking Computershare to amalgamate their holdings.
ADR program The company has a sponsored Level 1 American Depository Receipt ADR program, for which The Bank of New York acts as Depository.
A Level 1 ADR program is not listed on a US stock exchange and trades in the over-the-counter OTC market.
The ADR trading symbol is TYNLY and price quotes can be obtained from www.
One ADR represents 15 ordinary shares.
When dividends are paid to shareholders, the Depository makes the equivalent payment in US dollars to ADR holders.
For ADR shareholder enquiries, please contact: Shareholder Relations, The Bank of New York, PO Box 11248, Church Street Station, New York, NY 10286-1258, USA.
From the USA 1-888-BNY-ADRS toll free.
