Group Board Chairman John Hodson aged 56 has been with the Group since 1969.
In 1987 he was appointed a Director, and in 1993 he was appointed the Chief Executive of the Group.
He became Chairman on 1 January 2000.
He is also a Non-Executive Director of Intrinsic Value PLC.
Chief Operating Officer and Finance Director Michael Gibbins LVO aged 59 joined the Group as Finance Director on 1 July 1998, and was also appointed Chief Operating Officer on 1 January 2000.
Previously, he worked for KPMG both in the UK and overseas, and was admitted to partnership in 1976.
In 1996 he was appointed Comptroller to Diana, Princess of Wales.
Sir Harry Djanogly CBE aged 64 was appointed to the Board in 1987, and became Non-Executive Deputy Chairman on 1 January 2000.
Sir Harry is presently the Chairman of Coats PLC and a Non-Executive Director of Carpetright PLC.
He is a member of the Audit and Remuneration Committees.
Sir Brandon Gough aged 65 became a Non-Executive Director on 1 September 1999.
He is Chairman of fide La Rue PLC and of Montanaro UK Smaller Companies Investment Trust PLC and he was formerly Chairman of Coopers & Lybrand.
He is Chairman of the Audit Committee and a member of the Remuneration Committee.
Paul Selway-Swift aged 58 was appointed to the Board as a Non-Executive Director on 4 September 2000.
He is the Non-Executive Chairman of SVB Holdings PLC and a Non-Executive Director of Alba PLC.
He was formerly Deputy Chairman of HSBC Investment Bank and had been with the HSBC Group from 1962 until 1996.
He is a member of the Audit Committee and the Chairman of the Remuneration Committee.
