Stock Exchange Listings The ordinary shares of 25 pence each of the company are listed on the London Stock Exchange.
Ordinary shares of the company are also traded on the New York Stock Exchange in the form of American Depositary Shares and held in the form of American Depositary Receipts ADRs.
ADR holders receive the annual and interim reports issued to shareholders.
The company files a form 20-F each year with the United States Securities and Exchange Commission.
