With effect from 1st January 1998, 65% of the management fee including VAT and 65% of finance costs have been charged to capital.
This change to the allocation basis is in line with the Boards revised expected long-term split of returns from the Companys portfolio.
The comparative figures for prior years have been adjusted, where applicable, to reflect the capitalisation issue in March 1993 of one ordinary share for each ordinary share held.
