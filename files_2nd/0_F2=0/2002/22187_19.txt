Resolution 6 This resolution increases the authorised share capital of the company to 50,000,000.
During the year to 31 December 2002 part of the authorised but unissued share capital of the company was issued and allotted and, accordingly, the number of shares which remain available for issue by the company has been reduced.
The directors therefore consider it desirable to restore some additional headroom between the existing issued and authorised share capital.
An increase in the authorised share capital of the company to 50,000,000 would represent an increase in the companys authorised share capital of 25%.
Resolution 7 This resolution grants the directors authority to allot relevant securities up to an aggregate nominal amount of 10,922,600 being 1 33 3% of the companys issued share capital at 5 March 2003.
It is not the directors current intention to allot relevant securities pursuant to this resolution.
This authority replaces the existing authority to allot relevant securities but does not affect the ability to allot shares under the companys employee share schemes.
This authority will lapse on the earlier of the date falling five years from the passing of this resolution and the date of the annual general meeting of the company to be held in 2008.
Resolution 8 This is a special resolution whereby the directors seek power to allot shares for cash otherwise than in accordance with the pre-emption rights set out in section 89 1 of the Companies Act 1985 in connection with a rights issue or otherwise up to a maximum nominal amount of 1,638,390, being 5% of the companys issued share capital as at 5 March 2003.
