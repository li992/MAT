Accounting policies Basis of preparation Amlin plc the Company, domiciled in the United Kingdom, is the ultimate Parent Company for the Amlin Group the Group.
The separate financial statements of the Company are prepared as required by the Companies Act 2006.
The statement of financial position of the Company has also been prepared in accordance with International Financial Reporting Standards IFRSs and interpretations issued by the IFRS Interpretations Committee IFRICs, as adopted for use in the European Union EU.
In accordance with the exemption permitted under section 408 of the Companies Act 2006, the statement of profit or loss and statement of other comprehensive income of the Company are not presented as part of these financial statements.
