Dividends Accounting policy Dividends receivable from the Companys subsidiaries and joint venture investments are recognised only when they are approved or paid by shareholders.
Final dividend distributions to the Companys shareholders are recognised as a liability in the financial statements in the period in which they are approved by the Companys shareholders.
Interim dividends are recognised in the period in which they are paid.
