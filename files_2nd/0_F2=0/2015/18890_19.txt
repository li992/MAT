DILUTION LIMITS The Company operates its share schemes within the Investment Associations guidelines on dilution.
No share award may be granted if, as a result, the aggregate number of shares issued or transferred from treasury during the previous 10 years under all employee share schemes established by the Company would exceed 10% of the issued ordinary share capital ofthe Company on that date or, in the case of all discretionary employee share schemes, 5%.
