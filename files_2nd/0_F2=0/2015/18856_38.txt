Net income tax liabilities in the Group balance sheet include $112.7m relating to uncertain tax positions where management has had to exercise judgement in determining the most likely outcome in respect of the relevant issue.

Where corporate tax assets and liabilities are in the same jurisdictions, amounts are netted in the Group balance sheet.
