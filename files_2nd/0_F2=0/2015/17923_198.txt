6 Financial guarantees Under the terms of a deed poll guarantee, BHP Billiton Plc has guaranteed certain current and future liabilities of BHP Billiton Limited.
At 30 June 2015, the guaranteed liabilities amounted to US$13,487 million 2014: US$14,839 million.
BHP Billiton Plc and BHP Billiton Limited have severally, fully and unconditionally guaranteed the payment of the principal and premium, if any, and interest, including certain additional amounts which may be payable in respect of the notes issued by 100 per cent owned finance subsidiary BHP Billiton Finance USA Ltd. BHP Billiton Plc and BHP Billiton Limited have guaranteed the payment of such amounts when they become due and payable, whether on an interest payment date, at the stated maturity of the notes, by declaration or acceleration, call for redemption or otherwise.
At 30 June 2015, the guaranteed liabilities amounted to US$13,500 million 2014: US$15,500 million.
Further, BHP Billiton Plc and BHP Billiton Limited have severally guaranteed a Group Revolving Credit Facility of US$6,000 million 2014: US$6,000 million, which remains undrawn.
BHP Billiton Plc has further guaranteed US$375 million 2014: US$375 million relating to interest rate swap agreements entered into by 100 per cent owned finance subsidiary BHP Billiton Finance BV.
At 30 June 2015, the liability recognised for financial guarantees is US$ nil 2014: US$ nil.
