32 Employee benefits The Group operates both defined benefit and defined contribution pension schemes.
The pension charge for the defined contribution pension schemes for the year is 85.9m 2014: 78.7m.
In 2015, there was 1 main defined benefit pension scheme where the Group is the sole employing sponsor: The Capita Pension and Life Assurance Scheme Capita scheme.
Grouped together under Other schemes are smaller arrangements: 5 further schemes where the Group is the sole employing sponsor, 3 of which transferred to the Group as a result of the acquisitions of avocis during 2015: an allocated section of a Local Government Pension Scheme where the Group is the employing sponsor: a segregated section of a multi-employer scheme in which the Group is a participating employer: 2 segregated sections in industry wide schemes: and several schemes to which the Group makes contributions under Admitted Body status to our clients defined benefit pension schemes in respect of certain TUPE employees.
For the Admitted Body schemes, the Group will only participate in the schemes for a finite period up to the end of the contract with the client.
