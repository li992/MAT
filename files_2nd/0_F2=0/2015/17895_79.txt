Investment property Cost Land and buildings that are leased to non-Group entities are classied as investment property.
The Group measures investment property atitscost less accumulated depreciation and impairment losses.
Depreciation Depreciation is provided, on a straight-line basis, to write off the cost of investment property over its estimated useful life of up to 50years.
The assets residual values and useful lives are reviewed, and adjusted if appropriate, at each balance sheet date.
