Discontinued operations On 28 May 2014 the Group completed its formation of a new venture with China Resources Enterprise Limited CRE.
The new venture is classified as an associate within continuing operations.
In accordance with IFRS 5 Non-current Assets Held for Sale and Discontinued Operations, the Chinese operations for the period up to 28 May 2014 have been classified as a disposal group.
In addition the US operations, representing the remaining costs of the orderly restructuring process, continues to be classified as a disposal group.
