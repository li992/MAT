Dividend mandate Shareholders can arrange to have their dividends paid directly into their bank or building society account by completing a bank mandate form.
The advantages to using this service are: the payment is more secure than sending a cheque through the post: itavoids the inconvenience of paying in a cheque and there is no risk of lost, stolen or out-of-date cheques.
A mandate form can be obtained from Equiniti or you will find one on the reverse of the tax voucher of your last dividend payment.
