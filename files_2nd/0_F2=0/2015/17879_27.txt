Merger reserve This represents the difference between the fair value and the nominal value of shares issued in connection with the acquisition of AVEVA AB in 2004.
Own shares held Own shares held reserve represents the cost of the shares in AVEVA Group plc purchased in the open market and held by the AVEVA Group Employee Benefit Trust 2008 EBT to satisfy deferred shares under the Groups deferred annual bonus share plan.
