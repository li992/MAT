5.2 Share capital and buy-back programs On 28 August 2014, BHP Billiton Plc cancelled 24,113,658 ordinary shares of US$0.50 each held in treasury by BHP Billiton Plc.
Following the cancellation, no treasury shares are held by BHP Billiton Plc.
At the Annual General Meetings held in 2014, shareholders authorised BHP Billiton Plc to make on-market purchases of up to 211,207,180 of its ordinary shares, representing 10 per cent of BHP Billiton Plcs issued share capital at that time.
During FY2015, we did not make any on-market or off-market purchases of BHP Billiton Limited shares or BHP Billiton Plc shares under any share buy-back program.
As at the date of this Directors Report, there are no current on-market buy-backs.
Shareholders will be asked at the 2015 Annual General Meetings to renew this authority.
As at the date of this Directors Report, the Directors have no present intention to exercise this authority, if granted.
