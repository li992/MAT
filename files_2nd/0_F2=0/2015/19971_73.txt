Contingent liabilities The Group has potential obligations in respect of legal action following the 2012 acquisition of businesses in Nevada, USA.
The Group does not expect a material adverse outcome, but there can be no assurance that the results of this legal action will not have a material impact upon the Groups cash flows or results.
The value and timing of possible obligations in this regard are subject to a high degree of uncertainty and cannot be reliably measured.
Accordingly, no amounts are provided.
