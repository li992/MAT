Gas and liquids reserves unaudited The group has estimated proven and probable gas and liquid reserves in the UK and North America.
Estimates are made by management.
The principal fields in the UK are South Morecambe, North Morecambe, Galleon and the Rough field associated with Centrica Storage.
The principal fields in North America are Medicine Hat and Entice.
