Notes to the consolidated accounts Unilever Group 18 Deferred taxation Following the adoption of the new pension accounting standard FRS 17 with effect from 1 January 2003, deferred tax balances in respect of pensions are now reported as a separate component of the pensions balances and no longer aggregated with the rest of the deferred tax balances.
This treatment has resulted in a decrease of the opening deferred tax liability balance of 129 million and the deferred tax asset balance of 820 million.
