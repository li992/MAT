Shares were acquired in Centrica plc on the basis of one Centrica share for every British Gas plc share held at demerger.
Shares in Centrica plc, acquired on demerger, are treated as having a base cost for capital gains tax purposes calculated in accordance with taxation legislation of 64.25 pence each.
Share capital consolidation On 10 May 1999, the ordinary share capital of Centrica plc was consolidated on the basis of nine new ordinary shares 5 of 5 9 pence for every ten ordinary shares of 5 pence held on 7 May 1999.
The consolidation was linked to the payment of a special dividend of 12 pence per share on 23 June 1999.
