SP had net cash of 6.6m upon acquisition, giving an equity value of 55.7m for the transaction.

The acquisition was for an equity value of 66.0m, to be financed using the Companys existing debt facilities.
Softline had net cash of 11.1m as at 31 March 2003, giving an enterprise value of 54.9m.
Softline is a leading provider of business management software in South Africa and Australia.
