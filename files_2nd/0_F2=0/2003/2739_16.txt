70 Cairn Energy PLC Annual Report & Accounts 2003 Notice of Annual General Meeting Notice is hereby given that the Annual General Meeting of Cairn Energy PLC will be held in the Glamis Room at the Caledonian Hilton Hotel, Princes Street, Edinburgh, EH1 2AB on Tuesday 4 May 2004 at 12 noon for the following purposes: As Routine Business To consider and, if thought fit, pass the following resolutions, of which resolutions 1 to 10 will be proposed as ordinary resolutions.
1 That the report and accounts for the year ended 31 December 2003 be received.
2 That the directors remuneration report contained in the report and accounts be approved.
3 That Ernst & Young LLP be re-appointed as auditors and that the directors be authorised to fix their remuneration.
4 That Philip Tracy, who retires at the first Annual General Meeting following his appointment, be elected as a director.
5 That Todd Hunt, who retires at the first Annual General Meeting following his appointment, be elected as a director.
6 That Mark Tyndall, who retires at the first Annual General Meeting following his appointment, be elected as a director.
Mr Tyndall is a member of the remuneration committee.
7 That Hamish Grossart, who retires by rotation, be re-elected as a director.
Mr Grossart is a member of the audit, remuneration and nomination committees.
8 That Kevin Hart, who retires by rotation, be re-elected as a director.
9 That Malcolm Thoms, who retires by rotation, be re-elected as a director.
