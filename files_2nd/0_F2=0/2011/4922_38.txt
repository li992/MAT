Assets under construction Included in freehold land and buildings is an amount of 1,117,000 2010: 1,085,000 of capitalised interest, of which 54,000 2010: 231,000 was capitalised in the current period and included with additions.
This interest relates to the 25,000,000 revolving credit facility used to finance the building of the new commissary and head office.
Tax relief on capitalised interest is claimed in full in the period in which the interest is paid.
Accelerated depreciation and impairment As a result of the new Milton Keynes head office being completed at the end of November 2011, the Group reconsidered the residual value and remaining life of assets at the existing Milton Keynes head office as at 26 December 2010.
Consequently, the Group took an accelerate depreciation charge of 583,000.
