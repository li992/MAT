84 Great Portland Estates | Annual Report 2011 Notes forming part of the Company financial statements i Accounting policies Accounting convention The Company financial statements are prepared under UK GAAP and the historical cost convention as modified by the revaluation of investments in subsidiary undertakings.
The Company has prepared its financial statements on a going concern basis, see page 90.
Subsidiary undertakings and joint ventures Shares in subsidiary undertakings are carried at amounts equal to their original cost and any subsequent movement in the revaluation reserve of those subsidiaries, thus reflecting in the Companys balance sheet the surplus arising from the revaluation and the sale of investments and investment properties of those subsidiaries, limited to the value of the Group balance sheet.
Accounting policies for share-based payments, deferred tax and financial instruments are the same as those of the Group and are set out on pages 62 to 64. ii Profit attributable to members of the parent undertaking As permitted by section 408 Companies Act 2006, the Company has not presented its own profit and loss account.
