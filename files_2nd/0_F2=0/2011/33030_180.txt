Under that law they are required to prepare the consolidated financial statements in accordance with International Financial Reporting Standards IFRS as adopted by the European Union EU and applicable law and have elected to prepare the parent company financial statements in accordance with applicable United Kingdom law and United Kingdom accounting standards United Kingdom generally accepted accounting practice.
In preparing the consolidated financial statements the directors have also elected to comply with IFRSs as issued by the International Accounting Standards Board IASB.
