Events after balance sheet date 2011 Recommended all-share merger of equals between Glencore International plc and Xstrata plc The Glencore Directors and the Independent Xstrata Directors have reached agreement on the terms of a recommended all-share merger of equals of 37 Glencore and Xstrata plc.
The terms of the Merger will provide holders of scheme shares with 2.8 new Glencore shares for each Xstrata plc share held.
The merger will be effected by way of a Court sanctioned scheme of arrangement of Xstrata plc under Part 26 of the UK Companies Act, pursuant to which 8 Glencore will acquire the entire issued and to be issued ordinary share capital of Xstrata plc not already owned by the Glencore Group.
The merger is subject 26 to a number of conditions, including shareholder approval of both companies.
