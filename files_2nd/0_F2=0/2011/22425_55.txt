44 The City of London Investment Trust plc Annual Report and Financial Statements 2011 Notes to the Financial Statements continued 16 Risk management policies and procedures continued 16.4 Fair values of financial assets and financial liabilities The financial assets and financial liabilities are either carried in the balance sheet at their fair value or the balance sheet amount is a reasonable approximation of fair value due from brokers, dividends and interest receivable, due to brokers, accruals, cash at bank and bank overdrafts.
At 30 June 2011 the aggregate fair value of the debenture stocks was 58,234,000 2010: 58,110,000 and the aggregate fair value of the preferred and preference stock was 2,428,000 2010: 2,311,000.
These valuations are obtained from brokers based on market prices.
The debenture stock, preference stock and preferred ordinary stock are carried in the balance sheet at par.
