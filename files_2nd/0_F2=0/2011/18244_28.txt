The Group has five bank loan and mortgage facilities which contain change-of-control clauses.
Three of these facilities require the prior written consent of the lender to a change of control over the parent company, without which such change of control would constitute an event of default.
A change of control under the remaining two facilities would similarly constitute an event of default but no provision is made for the prior written consent of the lender.
