Finance income is recognised in the income statement on a straight line basis, reecting the periodic rate of return on the Groups net investment in the lease.
The Group acts as a lessor of a single finance lease, which is of 45 years duration and relates to the provision of student accommodation.
The other party, as lessee, is regarded to be the economic owner of the leased asset.
