25 Acquisitions On September 1, 2011, the Group acquired 100 per cent of the issued share capital of R. Brooks Associates Inc. a leading specialist civil nuclear reactor services business in the United States.
Of the US$30m acquisition cost net of cash acquired, US$12m has been allocated to identifiable intangible assets and US$5m to other assets and liabilities.
The balance of US$13m is recognised as goodwill.
