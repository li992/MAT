The Company has seed funded both Premier Alliance Trust Investment Funds Limited PATIF and Alliance Trust Investment Funds Limited ATIF.
Both are UK domiciled Open Ended Investment Companies OEICs.
During the year, management took the decision to combine the operation of the PATIF subfunds within the ATIF structure via a Scheme of Arrangement.
This transition was completed in October 2010, and PATIF is now in the process of being wound up.
