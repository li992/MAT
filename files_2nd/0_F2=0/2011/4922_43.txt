Business combinations On 26th April 2011, the Group acquired 75% of the ordinary shares of Intergrowth Enterprises Limited, the master franchisee for Dominos Pizza Germany.
On 9 June 2011, the company changed its name to DP Cyco Limited.
The acquisition has been accounted for using the purchase method of accounting.
The financial statements include the results of DP Cyco Limited and its wholly owned subsidiary Dominos Pizza Germany Gmbh known collectively as Dominos Germany for the period from the date of acquisition to the period end.
The non-controlling interest is measured at the proportionate share of net assets.
