American Depositary Receipts Johnson Matthey has a sponsored Level 1 American Depositary Receipt ADR programme which BNY Mellon administers and for which it acts as Depositary.
Each ADR represents two Johnson Matthey ordinary shares.
The ADRs trade on the US over-the-counter OTC market under the symbol JMPLY.
When dividends are paid to shareholders, the Depositary converts such dividends into US dollars, net of fees and expenses, and distributes the net amount to ADR holders.
