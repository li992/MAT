130 Five Year Summary 131 Shareholder Information Warning to shareholders boiler room share scams Over recent months a number of companies, including BBA Aviation plc, have become aware that some of their shareholders have received unsolicited telephone calls concerning their shares.
These calls are from organisations based outside the UK who, for example, offer to buy the shares for considerably more than the current market price.
Shareholders are advised to be very wary of any unsolicited advice or offers.
These operations are commonly known as boiler rooms.
Callers can be very persistent and extremely persuasive.
Shareholders are advised not to give details of their email addresses or other personal details to any third party that they do not know.
