Post balance sheet events On 1 September 2011, the Group completed the acquisition of 100% of the share capital of Transeals Pty Limited Transeals, a privately owned company based in Perth, Australia.
Transeals manufactures and distributes seals used in hydraulic equipment, currently serving the western parts of Australia.
This strategic acquisition allows the Hallite operation in Australia, which is mainly east coast based, to develop its aftermarket presence in the buoyant mining and oil and gas markets of Western Australia.
