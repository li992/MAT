SHARE-BASED PAYMENT In 2009 1,255,205 ordinary share awards with a par value of $0.0000125 each were awarded to Directors and senior management as part of the ipo plan.
The share awards are conditional on the employee completing three years service the vesting period from the date of the IPO, during which any dealings are prohibited.
These share awards are not subjected to any performance conditions.
The Group has no legal or constructed obligation to repurchase the shares related to the granted share awards.
During the year ended 31 December 2010 747,349 ordinary share awards were forfeited due to the termination of the employment with the Company of five senior managers, of which 302,880 share awards were granted to the new senior managers.
These share awards are also conditional on the completion of three years service with any dealings prohibited during that period.
During the year ended 31 December 2011 3,137,401 share awards were granted to the new senior managers out of the Employee Share Plan, of which 115,377 share awards subject to non-market conditions relating to the satisfactory performance of the duties and a three year vesting period and 3,022,024 share awards are not performance-related but subject to the completion of three years service with any dealings prohibited during that period.
