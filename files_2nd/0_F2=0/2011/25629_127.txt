The UK Government has announced various measures in relation to UK corporation tax including a 2% reduction in the headline rate of corporation tax from April 2011, and 1% in the three subsequent years to reduce the UK tax rate from 28% to 23%.
As at 31st December 2011 the 2% rate reduction to 26% is already in force and only the first subsequent 1% rate reduction has been substantively enacted.
Therefore the impact of the two remaining annual 1% reductions have not been incorporated into the income tax charge for the year ended 31st December 2011.
