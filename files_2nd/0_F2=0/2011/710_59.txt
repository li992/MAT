Allied Gold Mining PLC December 31, 2011 C. Share Based Compensation Audited Options The Company operates an Employee Incentive Option Scheme.
Directors do not participate in this scheme.
Under the rules of the London Stock Exchange, all options issued to Directors are required to be approved by the Companys shareholders.
As part of the Option Scheme of Arrangement implemented on June 30, 2011, all outstanding Allied Gold Limited options were transferred to Allied Gold PLC options, the new holding Company of the Allied Gold Group.
Each option is convertible into one ordinary share in Allied Gold PLC when exercised.
2,062,498 shares were issued pursuant to the exercise of options by Directors during the period excluding shares issued to Mr Steemson pursuant to the exercise of options after he resigned as a Director of the Company.
