An allowance is made for future improvements in annuitant mortality based on experience and externally published data.
Male annuitant mortality is assumed to improve in accordance with 100% of CMI2009 Working Paper 41, with a Long Term Rate of improvement of 1.5% for future experience, and 2.0% for statutory reserving.
Female annuitant mortality is assumed to improve in accordance with 100% of CMI2009, with a Long Term Rate of improvement of 1.0% for future experience and 1.5% for statutory reserving.
In each case, the annual improvement is assumed to reduce linearly after age 85 to zero at age 120.
