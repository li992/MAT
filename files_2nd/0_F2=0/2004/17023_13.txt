Financial instruments The policies and procedures used by the Group to manage its financial exposures, principally interest rate risk, are set out on page 26 of the Financial Review.
As permitted by FRS 13, short-term debtors and creditors are excluded from the analysis.
All of the Groups financial assets and liabilities are denominated in sterling, with the exception of Euro denominated cash at bank of 0.1 million 2003: 0.1 million and the US private placement of 150.3 million $235m.
These US dollar loans have been converted to Sterling through cross currency swaps which leaves the Group with no exposure to the US dollar.
Financial assets The Groups financial assets comprise cash at bank of 7.3 million 2003: 36.0 million.
Cash and short term deposits are placed with banks on a rolling basis earning interest at a floating rate based on LBID equivalents.
