 Accountants N. V. Rotterdam, The Netherlands Basis of audit opinion We conducted our audit in accordance with auditing standards 1 March 2005 generally accepted in the Netherlands.
Those standards require that we plan and perform the audit to obtain reasonable assurance about whether the accounts are free of material misstatement.
An audit includes examining, on a test basis, evidence supporting the amounts and disclosures in the accounts.
An audit also includes assessing the accounting principles used and significant estimates made by management, as well as evaluating the overall presentation of the accounts.
We believe that our audit provides a reasonable basis for our opinion.
