"20 PRIOR YEAR ADJUSTMENT - ESOP Group and Company The prior year adjustment relates to the adoption of UITF 38 ""Accounting for ESOP Trusts""."
This represents a change in presentation of investment in own shares which is now deducted from the profit and loss account reserve.
In the prior year, investment in own shares was included in fixed asset investments.
The effect of the change in accounting policy has been to reduce fixed asset investments and reserves by 2.3m at 28 September 2003.
There has been no effect on the reported results.
Cash inflows arising from the net sale of own shares by the share trust have been reclassified from capital expenditure and financial investment to financing.
