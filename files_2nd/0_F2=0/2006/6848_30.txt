NOTES TO THE ACCOUNTS FOR THE FIFTY-TWO WEEKS ENDED 30 APRIL 2006 17. Business combinations The group acquired 100% of the voting shares of TD Ridley & Sons Limited, predominantly a tenanted estate, on 4 July 2005.
The group also acquired 100% of the voting shares of The Belhaven Group Plc, a listed group consisting of brewery, and managed and tenanted estates, on 2 October 2005.
Goodwill has arisen primarily due to additional synergies expected and the difference between property book value and value-in-use.
The fair value of properties acquired was established following a review of properties that was carried out by qualified surveyors employed by the company.
Retained properties have been revalued at their existing use value and properties for disposal have been valued at their estimated fair value less costs to sell.
The value of other current assets and liabilities have been adjusted to amounts to be realised or paid respectively.
The post-acquisition impact has been extrapolated on a time-basis to show the annualised effect on group income statement of the acquisitions.
