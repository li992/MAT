NOTES TO THE COMPANY FINANCIAL STATEMENTS CONTINUED K Related party transactions GUS plc and other GUS related companies were related parties of Burberry Group plc until 13 December 2005 as GUS plc owned the majority shareholding in Burberry Group plc.
On 13 December 2005 Burberry Group plc demerged from GUS plc and became the ultimate parent Company of the Group.
Share repurchase programme As part of the Share repurchase programme, 13,063,825 Ordinary Shares were purchased by the Company from GUS, representing a total cost, including expenses, of 52.2m.
All of the Ordinary Shares purchased from GUS have been cancelled.
In addition, 570,030 Ordinary Shares purchased from GUS in the year to 31 March 2005 were cancelled in the current year.
