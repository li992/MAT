Consolidated accounts Notes to the accounts for the year ended 31 March 2006 continued 34 Acquisitions a 2006 i Scottish Radio Holdings plc On 9 August 2005 Emap plc completed the acquisition of the remaining 72.9% shareholding in Scottish Radio Holdings plc SRH not already owned by Emap for a cash consideration of 280m including costs of 8m and loan notes of 17m.
Also on 9 August 2005, Emap plc sold SRHs press business, Score Press Limited, to Johnston Press plc for 155m, settled as 136m in cash and the assumption of 19m of debt.
There was no profit or loss on the disposal of Score Press Limited.
The impact of the acquisition on the consolidated balance sheet was: Net book Fair value values adjustments Fair value m m m Intangible xed assets 107 3 104 Other xed assets 9 9 Asset held for sale 47 89 136 Current assets 22 2 20 Current liabilities 13 1 14 Long-term liabilities 7 31 38 Cash and overdrafts 26 26 191 Fair value of assets acquired 72.9% 139 Goodwill 158 Fair value of consideration 297 Comprising: Net cash 280 Loan notes 17 The principal fair value adjustments include 89m to Asset held for sale to restate SRHs interest in Score Press Limited to its fair value of 136m, the agreed sale price to Johnston Press plc of 155m, net of 19m of debt, and the recognition of a deferred taxation liability on the acquired intangible assets.
The goodwill arising on the acquisition of SRH is principally attributable to the workforce, certain customer relationships which did not meet the criteria for separate recognition as an intangible asset, and the anticipated operating synergies from the combination of the business with Emaps existing radio business.
SRH contributed 2m of operating profit after amortisation for the period since acquisition.
