18 Retirement benefit plans The companys employees participate in the Findel Group Pension Scheme, a dened benefit pension scheme with the assets held in separate trustee administered funds.
As the company is unable to identify its share of the underlying assets and liabilities in the scheme on a consistent and reasonable basis, it has taken advantage of the exemption afforded by FRS 17 Retirement benets to treat the pension scheme as if it were a dened contribution scheme.
The disclosures required regarding the assets and liabilities of the scheme can be found in Note 35 to the group financial statements.
