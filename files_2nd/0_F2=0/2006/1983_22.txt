This offers shareholders secure access to their account details held on the share register to amend address information and payment instructions directly, as well as providing a simple and convenient way of buying and selling the companys ordinary shares.

The best way to ensure that dividends are received as quickly as possible is to instruct the companys registrars to pay them directly into a bank or building society account: tax vouchers are then mailed to shareholders separately.
Dividend mandate forms are available from the registrars.
This method also avoids the risk of dividend cheques being delayed or lost in the post.
