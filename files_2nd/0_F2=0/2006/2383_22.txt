The Innovene operations represented a separate major line of business for BP.
As a result of the sale, these operations were treated as discontinued operations for the year ended 31 December 2005.
A single amount was shown on the face of the income statement comprising the post-tax result of discontinued operations and the post-tax loss recognized on the remeasurement to fair value less costs to sell and on disposal of the discontinued operation.
That is, the income and expenses of Innovene are reported separately from the continuing operations of the BP group.
