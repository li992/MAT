Dividends continued The second interim dividend for 2006 of 6.71 US cents per Ordinary Share was declared by the Board on 8 February 2007 and will be paid on 11 May 2007 to shareholders on the Register of Members on 20 April 2007.
All shareholders will receive the Sterling equivalent of 3.41 pence per Ordinary Share.
The estimated cost of this dividend will be $63m and it will be recognised as a liability in the Groups accounts on the declarationdate.
