EFFECT OF FIRST TIME ADOPTION OF IAS 32 AND 39 The Group adopted IAS 32 and IAS 39 from 1 May 2005 and has taken the exemption under IFRS 1 not to restate comparatives for IAS 32 and 39.
The effect of adoption of IAS 32 and IAS 39 was to create a financial asset of 48,000 and a financial liability of 71,000.
The fair value of financial assets and liabilities on transition is taken to a cash flow hedge reserve.
