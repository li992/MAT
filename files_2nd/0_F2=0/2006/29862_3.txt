The results for the year ended 30 April 2005 have been restated in accordance with IFRS.
The main differences as a result of adopting IFRS are: Investments are valued on a bid basis, as opposed to a mid basis: and Only dividends paid during the year are reflected in the Financial Statements.
The effects of adopting IFRS are disclosed in further detail within the Reconciliation of IFRS to UK GAAP on pages 42 and 43.
Total Earnings per share for the year ended 30 April 2006 and 30 April 2005, calculated in accordance with IFRS are disclosed in the Financial Summary on page 2.
