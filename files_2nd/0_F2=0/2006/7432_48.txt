Transition to IFRS For all periods up to and including the period ended 30 April 2005, the Group prepared its financial statements in accordance with UK GAAP.
These consolidated financial statements for the 52 weeks ended 29 April 2006 are the first that the Group has been required to prepare under IFRS as adopted by the EU.
Accordingly the Group has prepared financial statements that comply with IFRS applicable for the current accounting period and the significant accounting policies are disclosed on pages 42 to 44.
In preparing these financial statements, the Group has restated its opening balance sheet at 25 April 2004, the Groups date of transition to IFRS, and made those changes in accounting policies required by IFRS 1 First Time Adoption of International Financial Reporting Standards.
This note explains the adjustments made by the Group and the Company in restating the UK GAAP balance sheet as at 24 April 2004 and the financial statements for the period ended 30 April 2005.
In accordance with IFRS 1 First Time Adoption of International Financial Reporting Standards, the Group elected not to restate comparative information for the impact of IAS 32 Financial Instruments: Disclosure and Presentation and IAS 39 Financial Instruments: Recognition and Measurement.
These standards have been adopted from 1 May 2005 and the opening balance sheet has been adjusted to reect the adoption of these standards from that date.
Details of these adjustments are set out at the end of this note.
