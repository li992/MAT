Asset held for sale The asset held for sale at 30 December 2006 is the Carricks bakery in Newcastle upon Tyne.
Contracts were exchanged for the disposal of this property before the end of the year, conditional upon the purchaser being granted planning permission to redevelop the site.
This permission was granted in February 2007 and the disposal was completed then.
