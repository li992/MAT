75 Inchcape plc Annual report and accounts 2006 14 Available for sale financial assets continued Equity securities principally comprise the acquisition of 18.55% of the share capital of European Motor Holdings plc in December 2006.
The Group obtained control of this business on 29 January 2007, from which date the results will be consolidated as a subsidiary note 32.
These equity securities are not subject to interest rates and do not have fixed maturity dates.
At year end, they were valued by reference to traded market values.
At 31 December 2006 the bonds attracted a weighted average fixed interest rate of 4.9% 2005 5.4% and had a face value of 13.6m 2005 12.6m.
The bonds are traded on active markets with coupons generally paid on an annual basis.
