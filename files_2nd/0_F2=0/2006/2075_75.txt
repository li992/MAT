These drivers are our people: our licence to operate: our world-class assets: the way we do business: our financial strength and discipline: our project pipeline: and growth options.
Underpinning our strategic drivers are the values that guide us.
They are: An overriding commitment to health, safety, environmental responsibility and sustainable development.
Integrity and doing what we say we will do.
A commitment to achieving superior business results and stretching our capabilities.
Having the courage to lead change in the face of adversity.
The embracing of diversity and showing respect for and trust in each other.
