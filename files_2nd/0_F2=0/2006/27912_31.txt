b Paragon Performance Share Plan Awards under this plan comprise a right to acquire ordinary shares of 10p each in the Company for nil or nominal payment and will vest on the third anniversary of their granting, to the extent that the applicable performance criteria have been satisfied, if the holder is still employed by the Group.
The awards will lapse to the extent that the performance condition has not been satisfied on the third anniversary.
No part of an award vests for below median performance, 25% vests for median performance and 100% vests for upper quartile performance.
Between median and upper quartile performance, awards vest on a straight line basis.
