FINANCE LEASE RECEIVABLES The Group's finance lease receivables are car finance loans.
The average contractual life of such loans is 53 months, but it is likely that a significant proportion of customers will choose to settle their obligations early.
The contractual interest rate inherent in these leases is fixed at the outset.
The average effective interest rate in these contracts is 7.8% per annum.
