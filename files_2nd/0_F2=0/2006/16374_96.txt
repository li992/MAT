33 Events after the balance sheet date On 1 January 2007, Unilever completed the restructuring of its Portuguese businesses.
The result of the reorganisation is that Unilever now has a 55% share of the combined Portuguese entity, called Unilever Jernimo Martins.
The combined business includes the foods and home and personal care businesses.
The remaining 45% interest is held by Jernimo Martins Group.
The structure of the agreement is such that there is joint control of the newly formed entity and so it will be accounted for by Unilever as a joint venture.
