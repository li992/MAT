The BHP Billiton Strategic Framework comprises seven strategic drivers: People, our Licence to Operate, World-class Assets, the BHP Billiton Way, Financial Strength and Discipline, our Project Pipeline and Growth Options.
These drivers encompass our whole business and set the benchmarks against which we measure our performance.
BHP Billiton is a Dual Listed Company comprising BHP Billiton Limited and BHP Billiton Plc.
The two entities continue to exist as separate companies but operate as a combined group known as BHP Billiton.
