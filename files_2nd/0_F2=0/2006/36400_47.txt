Great Portland Estates Annual Report 2006 Notes forming part of the Group financial statements 61 21 Dividends The proposed final dividend of 7.33 pence per share 2005: 7.17 pence per share was approved by the Board on 22 May 2006 and is payable on 11 July 2006 to shareholders on the register on 2 June 2006.
The dividend is not recognised as a liability at 31 March 2006.
This brings the interim and final dividend for the year ended 31 March 2006 to 11 pence per share.
The 2005 final dividend of 11.6 million and the 2006 interim dividend of 5.9 million were paid in the period and are included within the Group Reconciliation of Other Movements in Equity.
