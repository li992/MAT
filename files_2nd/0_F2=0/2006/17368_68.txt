Interest rate risk The Group is exposed to interest rate risk on both interest-bearing assets and interestbearing liabilities.
The Group has a policy of actively managing its interest rate risk exposure while recognising that xing rates on all its debt eliminates the possibility of bene ting from rate reductions and similarly, having all its debt at oating rates unduly exposes the Group to increases in rates.
Liquidity risk The Group actively maintains a mixture of longand short-term committed facilities that are designed to ensure the Group has suf cient available funds to meet current and forecast financial requirements as cost-effectively as possible.
As at 31 December 2006 the Group has a committed credit facility of 817 million which was undrawn.
