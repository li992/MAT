Contingent liabilities continued Legal proceedings Investec is party to various legal proceedings, the ultimate resolution of of which is not expected to have a material adverse effect on the financial position of the group.
Following falls in the value of assets in the split capital investment trust sector, Carr Sheppards Crosthwaite Ltd CSC, in common with other firms in the industry, has received a number of complaints.
Each complaint has been investigated in accordance with CSC's complaints procedures and within guidelines set by the applicable regulatory authority.
A number of complaints are still in the process of adjudication by the Financial Ombudsman Service.
Meanwhile in December 2004 a group of firms, of which CSC is not a member, together agreed to make a contribution of 194 million towards the settlement of claims received to date, a reliable estimate cannot yet be made of any compensation payable by CSC in respect of this issue.
As part of the arrangements for the sale of CSC to Rensburg plc, Investec 1 Limited has indemnified Rensburg plc for any loss CSC incurs in relation to this matter.
