ASSETS AND LIABILITIES CLASSIFIED AS HELD FOR SALE The Group has classied its Kalixa business as held for sale.
This business, a fully integrated digital payments company, was transferred to held for sale as at 31 March 2016 after its acquisition as part of the bwin.
Certain of the assets of the Kalixa business relating to a beneficial shareholding in Visa Europe were realised in the year on the sale of that business to Visa Inc. Management have agreed a sale of the majority of the remaining operating Kalixa business and believe a disposal in the first quarter of 2017 will be achieved.
The remainder of the business is also being actively pursued for a disposal during 2017.
During the year, the Group disposed of its joint venture investment in Conspo which has also previously been classied as held for sale.
Proceeds of 16.4m including deferred consideration of 1.9m gave rise to a profit on disposal of 12.4m after professional costs.
All remaining assets and liabilities held for sale relate to the Kalixa business.
