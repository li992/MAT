Restricted cash The Group maintains bank accounts with the Groups principal bankers which are segregated from operating funds and which contain funds held on behalf of merchants, representing pooled merchant funds.
In addition, in compliance with the Financial Conduct Authority FCA rules and regulations, the Group holds qualifying liquid assets at least equal to the amounts owing to members.
These amounts are maintained in accounts which are segregated from operating funds.
Balances in the segregated accounts are maintained at a sufficient level to fully offset amounts owing to the Groups merchants and members.
As a legal right to offset exists between the balances owing and the cash balances segregated in the member and merchant accounts, only the net balance of surplus cash is disclosed on the consolidated statement of financial position as restricted cash.
