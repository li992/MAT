Accordingly, in the year ended 31 December 2015 the Company has undergone transition from reporting under International Financial Reporting Standards IFRS adopted by the European Union to FRS 101 issued by the FRC.
The financial statements have therefore been prepared in accordance with FRS 101.
This transition is not considered to have had a material effect on the financial statements.
As permitted by FRS 101, the Company has taken advantage of the disclosure exemptions available under that standard in relation to presentation of a cash flow statement, the reconciliation of net cash from operations, and related party transactions.
Where relevant, equivalent disclosures have been given in the Group accounts of the Company.
The accounting policies and financial risk management policies and objectives, where relevant to the Company, are consistent with those of the consolidated Group as set out in notes 2 and 3 to the consolidated financial statements.
