Related parties Key management personnel The Groups key management personnel, and persons connected with them, are considered to be related parties for disclosure purposes.
Key management personnel are defined as those persons having authority and responsibility for planning, directing and controlling the activities of the Group.
The Directors and members of the Executive Leadership Team are considered to be the key management personnel for disclosure purposes.
