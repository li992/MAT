44 Marshalls plc Annual Report and Accounts 2016 CORPORATE GOVERNANCE Going concern The Directors have adopted the going concern basis in preparing these Financial Statements in accordance with the Financial Reporting Councils Guidance on Risk Management, Internal Control and Related Financial and Business Reporting, issued in September 2014.
The Directors considered that it was appropriate to do so, having reviewed any uncertainties that may affect the Companys ability to continue as a going concern for at least the next 12 months from the date these Financial Statements were approved.
