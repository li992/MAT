Investments shares in Group undertakings continued During the year ended 31 March 2016, the issued ordinary share capital of a group undertaking was transferred to the Company from a subsidiary undertaking for a consideration of US$1,368.1m.
During March 2016, the transferred company commenced voluntary liquidation.
This process is expected to be completed later in 2016 and should result in no material gain or loss to the Company.
These transactions arose in connection with a Group reorganisation.
During the year ended 31 March 2015, the Company subscribed for additional shares in Experian Investment Holdings Limited EIHL at a cost of US$865.0m, with the consideration satisfied by the release of an amount owing by EIHL, and for shares in a newly incorporated subsidiary undertaking, Experian Holdings UK Limited EHUKL, at a cost of US$0.1m.
The Company subsequently transferred its investment in EIHL to EHUKL at market value and recognised a profit of US$7,967.9m see note H. These transactions arose in connection with a Group reorganisation.
