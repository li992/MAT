Inventory Our inventory primarily consists of mobile handsets and is presented net of an allowance for obsolete products.
Accounting policies Inventory is stated at the lower of cost and net realisable value.
Cost is determined on the basis of weighted average costs and comprises direct materials and, where applicable, direct labour costs and those overheads that have been incurred in bringing the inventories to their present location and condition.
