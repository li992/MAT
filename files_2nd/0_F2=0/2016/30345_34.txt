Have you been Contacted out of the blue Promised tempting returns and told the investment is safe Called repeatedly, or Told the offer is only available for a limited time?
If so, you might have been contacted by fraudsters.
Reject cold calls If youve been cold called with an offer to buy or sell shares, chances are its a high-risk investment or a scam.
You should treat the call with extreme caution.
The safest thing to do is to hang up.
