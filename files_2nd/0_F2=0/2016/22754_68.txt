Share-based payments continued Croda International Share Incentive Plan SIP The SIP was established in 2003 and has similar objectives to the Sharesave scheme in terms of increasing employee retention and share ownership.
Under the SIP scheme, employees enter into an agreement to purchase shares in the Company eachmonth.
Foreach share purchased by an employee, the Company awards a matching share which passes to the employee afterthree years service.
The matching shares are allocated each month at market value with this fair value charge being recognised inthe income statement in full in the year of allocation.. 23.
