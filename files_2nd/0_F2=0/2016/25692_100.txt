177 NOTES ON THE ACCOUNTS for the year ended 31st March 2016 36 Precious metal operating leases The group leases, rather than purchases, precious metals to fund temporary peaks in metal requirements provided market conditions allow.
These leases are from banks for specified periods typically a few months and for which the group pays a fee.
These arrangements are classified as operating leases.
The group holds sufficient precious metal inventories to meet all the obligations under these lease arrangements as they fall due.
At 31st March 2016 precious metal leases were 70.3 million 2015 18.7 million.
