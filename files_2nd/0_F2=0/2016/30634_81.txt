Vodafone Group Plc Annual Report 2016 Notes to the consolidated financial statements continued 16.
Trade and other payables Our trade and other payables mainly consist of amounts we owe to our suppliers that have been invoiced or are accrued.
They also include taxes and social security amounts due in relation to our role as an employer.
Derivative financial instruments with a negative market value are reported within this note.
Accounting policies Trade payables are not interest bearing and are stated at their nominal value.
