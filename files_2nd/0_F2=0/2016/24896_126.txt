Business combinations The Group has incurred a net cash outflow in respect of business combinations and disposals of 5.3m in the year.
On 12 October 2015 Homeserve Membership Limited, a Group company, acquired 100% of the issued share capital and obtained control of Home Energy Services Limited, a gas services business based in Nottingham.
The acquisition enhances the Groups gas network.
