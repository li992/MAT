WH Smith PLC Strategic Corporate Financial Other Annual report and accounts 2016 103 report governance statements information Warning to Shareholders Boiler Room Scams In recent years, many companies have become aware that their shareholders have received unsolicited phone calls or correspondence concerning investment matters.
These are typically from overseas-based brokers who target UK shareholders, offering to sell them what often turn out to be worthless or high risk shares in US or UK investments.
These operations are commonly known as boiler rooms.
Information on how to avoid share fraud or report a scam can be found on our website at www.
You can also call the Financial Conduct Authority Consumer Helpline on 0800 111 6768 or go to www.
