We are a FTSE 250 company listed on the London Stock Exchange, with over 3,000 employees serving families and local communities across the United Kingdom for generations.
At 30 December 2016 Dignity owned 792 funeral locations and operated 44 crematoria in the United Kingdom.
We continue to have a strong market presence in pre-arranged funeral plans, where people plan and pay for their funeral in advance.
This report demonstrates how our clear and consistent strategy, strong governance, dedicated people and financial performance combine to create and sustain value for all our stakeholders our clients, employees, shareholders and the local communities we serve.
