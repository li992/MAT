The Insurance Claims provision represents managements best estimate of claims under the General, Marine and Employers Liability policies.
Settlement of such claims is dependent on negotiation and, potentially, litigation with third parties, the timing of which cannot be predicted with complete accuracy.
The Employers National Insurance on Share Option provision is calculated in accordance with IFRS 2 Share-based Payment in respect of the Executive Share Option Scheme and the LTIP share option plans.
Settlement of the provision follows the maturity date of the LTIP options.
The provision for Maintenance of Quay Walls is made to provide for a contractual obligation in accordance with the terms and conditions relating to the contract for sale of certain property at the Port of Dundee.
It is expected that the provision will be used within the next year.
The provision for Abandonment of River Structures relates to removal of structures in the Thames near Tilbury.
