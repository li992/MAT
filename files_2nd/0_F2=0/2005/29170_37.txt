17 Related Parties The affairs of the Company and its subsidiary, SAL, are managed in conjunction with those of the Alliance Trust Group.
Although the Company and the Alliance Trust are not controlled by common shareholders, the composition of each board is currently the same and for the purposes of Financial Reporting Standard 8 the companies are regarded as related parties, requiring disclosure of material, mutual transactions.
The companies operate from the same office in Dundee, employ staff jointly and share the cost of common services.
The basis of allocation is 25% for the Company and 75% for the Alliance Trust after allowing for a contribution from ATS and reflects the respective sizes of the companies.
During the year to 31 July 2005 the Second Alliance Trust paid a contribution of 1,228,000 887,000.
During the year the Company sold to ATF, at market value, 1,839,374 of preference stocks held in the Companys portfolio.
SAL has a deposit facility with ATS.
