Contingent liabilities The Group has a contingent liability arising from a performance guarantee that Land Securities PLC has given, severally with its Telereal joint venture partner, for the performance by Telereal Services Limited of its service obligations to BT together with a guarantee related to transaction issues associated with the BT outsourcing contract.
The Groups maximum liability under the guarantee is 50.0m plus a further amount which is capped by reference to amounts either distributed or available for distribution to each shareholder by certain of the Telereal companies up to a further 50.7m.
At 31 March 2005, the estimated amount of the Groups exposure to the guarantee was approximately 100.7m 2004: 100.7m.
