Annual report 2005 Notes 33 Contingent liabilities and financial commitments On 9 August 2005 the Group entered into a Tax Deed of Covenant, the primary objective of which is to ensure that the Group does not trigger a total fide-grouping liability of approximately 173.2m.
This would arise in the event of W&DB Pubs Limited being sold outside of the Group within six years for corporation tax purposes and three years for stamp duty land tax purposes.
The Directors consider the likelihood of such a sale to be remote.
The Company has provided guarantees to Barclays Bank PLC in relation to loans entered into by tenants to finance the purchase of certain fixtures and fittings.
The total amount guaranteed under these arrangements is 1.1m 2004: nil.
