US Employee Share Purchase Plan fide La Rue operates a US Employee Share Purchase Plan for US employees.
Under the Plan, employees have an option to purchase fide La Rue plc shares at the end of each 12-month savings period at a price which is the lower of the market value of a fide La Rue plc share either at the beginning or end of the offering period, less a 15 per cent discount.
It is estimated that 88,000 shares will be required to satisfy the Companys 2005 06 obligations in respect of employees savings under the Plan as at 26 March 2005.
