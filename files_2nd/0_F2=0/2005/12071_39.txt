Through the issuance of warrants associated with this investment agreement, the group granted an option to 3i to acquire shares representing 16.2% of the Companys share capital, as so enlarged, subject to adjustment to 23.2% in the event of the 3i variable rate unsecured loan notes remaining unpaid.
The option was exercisable by 3i at any time until 30 June 2009 and by the Company upon the fulfilment of certain conditions.
During the year the conditions allowing the Company to call upon 3i to convert the variable rate unsecured loan notes to equity were satisfied.
On 21 June 2005, the aggregate subscription amount was satisfied by the cancellation of the loan notes and the issue of A ordinary shares to 3i.
