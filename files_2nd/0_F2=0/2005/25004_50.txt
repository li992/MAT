This includes $193m of fixed rate debt with a 10-year maturity which the Group has an option to repay after five years and a $32m floating rate component that can be repaid after two years.
On 8 April 2005 it was decided that ICAP would exercise its option to satisfy 2.3m $4.3m of the final deferred consideration payment in respect of the First Brokers acquisition in cash rather than shares.
These shares were included within the balance sheet as contingent share capital note 15.
