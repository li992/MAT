40 Share-Based Payments The Group Share Options, Share Matching and Long-Term Incentive Plans provide for a grant price equal to the average quoted market price of the Group shares on the date of grant.
The vesting period is generally three years.
The options expire if they remain unexercised after the exercise period has lapsed.
Furthermore, options are forfeited if the employee leaves the Group before the options vest.
The options are equity settled.
