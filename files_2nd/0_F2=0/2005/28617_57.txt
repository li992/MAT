Prior year adjustments The comparative figures have been restated for the effects of the following changes in accounting policies.
Pension liability In accordance with the requirements of FRS 17 Retirement Benefits, the Company has recognised the deficit on its defined benefit pension schemes as a liability in the balance sheet.
Changes to the value of the surplus or deficit on the schemes are recognised in full in equity.
The obligation is shown net of related deferred tax, which has been recognised directly in reserves.
Under the previous accounting policy, the cost to the Company of the schemes was charged to the profit and loss account over the expected working lives of the scheme members.
This change has reduced the Companys reported profit for the year ended 31 December 2004 by 728,000, and has reduced shareholders funds at 31 December 2004 by 11,018,000.
Share based payments In accordance with FRS 20 Share Based Payments, the Company has recognised an expense in relation to the fair value of outstanding share options and share awards in relation to the Groups Share Incentive Plan SIP and Long Term Incentive Plan granted to employees after 7 November 2002.
These charges are spread over the vesting period of the relevant awards.
Under the previous accounting policy no expense was recognised in relation to share option awards but charges were made for share awards granted before 7 November 2002.
In relation to the SIP free shares, the charge was recognised in the performance year.
This change has decreased shareholders funds at 31 December 2004 by 78,000 and increased profit for the year then ended by 1,283,000.
Dividends In accordance with FRS 21 Events after the balance sheet date, the Company recognises final dividends as a liability in the balance sheet when they are approved.
Under the previous accounting policy, a liability was recognised when the dividend was proposed.
This change has increased the reported profit for the year ended 31 December 2004 by 4,796,000, and has increased shareholders funds at 31 December 2004 by 6,948,000.
Fixed asset investments In accordance with FRS 25 Financial Instruments: Disclosure and Presentation, and FRS 26 Financial Instruments: Measurement, the Company has classified its investment in equity securities as available for sale and has recorded them in the balance sheet at their fair value.
Changes in fair value are taken straight to reserves.
Under the previous accounting policy, equity investments were reported at historical cost.
This change has increased shareholders funds at 31 December 2004 by 782,000 but has had no impact on the reported profit for the year then ended.
Taxation Tax related balances have been restated to take account of the tax effect of the above changes.
