Events after the balance sheet date On 12 January 2006, the Group agreed to acquire the investment management and private banking business of Dexia Banque Internationale Luxembourg S. A. London Branch.
The business will be transferred to Rathbone Investment Management Limited by way of a Court order sanctioning a banking business transfer scheme pursuant to Part VII of the Financial Services and Markets Act 2000.
The acquisition is subject to regulatory and Court approval.
The Court hearing is scheduled for 23 March 2006 and it is expected that completion will take place during April 2006.
The business to be acquired consists of approximately 50 million in loans and 20 million of deposits in addition to uninvested capital balances from client portfolios of approximately 30 million : and is being purchased at book value.
The consideration payable, made up of initial and deferred amounts, will be a maximum amount of 14 million and is ultimately dependent on the value of the funds under management transferred.
