Pillar Property PLC Annual report and accounts 2005 Glossary 52 CLOUT City of London Ofce Unit Trust, a Jersey based closed ended property unit trust.
Earnings per Share EPS profit after taxation attributable to ordinary shareholders divided by the weighted average number of ordinary shares in issue during the year.
Equivalent Yield A weighted average of the initial yield and reversionary yield and represents the return a property will produce based upon the timing of the income received.
In accordance with usual practice, the equivalent yield as determined by the groups external valuers assumes rent received annually in arrears and on gross values including prospective purchasers costs.
Estimated Rental Value ERV The groups external valuers opinion as to the market rent, which, on the date of valuation, could reasonably be expected to be obtained on a new letting or rent review of a property.
Forward Funded Commitment A funding commitment to purchase a development at a point in time which has been agreed between the purchaser and the seller.
Triggers to purchase could for example include practical completion of the scheme or the letting of the individual retail units.
Funds under Management Investment properties in HUT, HIF, CLOUT and PREF.
Gearing gross Total borrowings, including bank overdrafts as a percentage of net assets.
Gearing net Total borrowings, including bank overdrafts, less short-term deposits and cash as a percentage of net assets.
GIA Gross internal retail area of units within a scheme.
Group Pillar Property PLC and its subsidiaries excluding joint ventures.
HIF Hercules Income Fund, a Jersey based closed ended property unit trust.
HUT Hercules Unit Trust, a Jersey based closed ended property unit trust.
Initial Yield The annualised net rents generated by the portfolio or property expressed as a percentage of the portfolio valuation, excluding development properties.
Interest Cover The number of times group net interest payable is covered by group rental income.
Interest Rate Swap A financial instrument where two parties agree to exchange xed and oating interest rate obligations for a predetermined amount of time.
An independent information business which supplies market indices and portfolio benchmarks to the property industry.
Joint Venture An entity in which the group holds an interest on a long-term basis and is jointly controlled by the group and one or more co-venturers under a contractual arrangement whereby decisions on financial and operating policies essential to the operation, performance and financial position of the venture require each co-venturers consent.
Market Value The estimated amount for which a property should exchange on the date of valuation between a willing buyer and a willing seller in an arms length transaction after proper marketing wherein the parties had each acted knowingly, prudently and without compulsion.
Open A1 No restrictions on the type of goods that can be sold.
Passing Rent The gross rent received, less any ground rent payable under head leases.
PREF Pillar Retail Europark Fund, a closed ended Luxembourg based Fonds Communde Placement FCP.
Pre-let A lease signed with an occupier prior to completion of a development.
Retail Park A scheme with at least 30,000 sq ft GIA, solely comprised of retail warehouse units.
They have a minority of units occupied by traditional high street comparison goods retailers.
Reversion The increase in rent estimated by the groups external valuers, where the passing rent is below the current estimated rental value.
The increases to rent arise on rent reviews, letting of vacant space and expiry of rent free periods.
Reversionary Yield The anticipated yield, which the initial yield will rise to once the rent reaches the estimated rental value.
Shopping Park A scheme with at least 30,000 sq ft GIA, comprising six or more traditional high street occupiers who account for at least 50% of the area of the scheme.
