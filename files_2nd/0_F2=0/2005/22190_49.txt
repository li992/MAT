Dened contribution post-employment benefit plans The group operates a number of dened contribution personal pension plans for new employees and for existing employees who are not members of the dened benefit scheme.
The expense recognised for the dened contribution plans is 1.0 million 2004: 1.1 million.
Dened benefit post-employment benefit plan The group operates a funded dened benefit scheme for certain employees, providing benets based on final salary.
The assets of the scheme are held in a separate trustee-administered fund.
Contributions to the scheme are assessed in accordance with the advice of an independent qualied actuary using the projected unit method.
The scheme was closed to new applicants from 1998.
