The cover is printed on Revive 100 silk which is made from 100% post consumer waste.
The text is printed on Amadeus 50 Silk which is produced using 50% recycled post-consumer waste and 50% wood fibre from fully sustainable forests with FSC certification.
All pulps used are Elemental Chlorine Free ECF.
Printed in the UK by Pureprint who are a Carbon Neutral company.
Both the manufacturing mill andthe printer are registered to the Environmental Management System ISO14001 and are Forest Stewardship FSC chain-of-custody certified.
