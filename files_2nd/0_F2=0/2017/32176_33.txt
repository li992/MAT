STRATEGIC REPORT GOVERNANCE FINANCIAL STATEMENTS USEFUL INFORMATION Additional Information Calculation of changes in constant currency Where changes in constant currency are presented in this statement, they are calculated by retranslating current year results at prior year exchange rates.
This represents a change to the methodology applied in previous years, which involved retranslating prior year results at current year exchange rates.
This change, which has not had a material impact, has been made to align with how the majority of external stakeholders view constant currency performance comparisons.

Absolute numbers presented in the tables are rounded for presentational purposes, whereas the growth percentages are calculated on unrounded numbers.
