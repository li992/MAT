Equity settled share based payments that are made to the employees of the Companys subsidiaries are treated as increases in equity over the vesting period of the award, with a corresponding increase in the Companys investments in subsidiaries, based on an estimate of the number of shares that will eventually vest.
This is the only addition to investment in subsidiaries in the current year.
