Annual Report & Accounts 2017 Glossary The Directors measure the performance of the Group based on a range of financial measures, including measures not recognised by EU-adopted IFRS.
These alternative performance measures may not be directly comparable with other companies alternative performance measures and the Directors do not intend these to be a substitute for, or superior to, IFRS measures.
Terms are listed in alphabetical order.
Adjusted Earnings Per Share The calculation of basic and diluted earnings per share is detailed in Note 10.
Adjusted basic and diluted earnings per ordinary share have been based on the profit for the period attributable to equity holders of the parent for each financial period but excluding the post-tax effect of certain exceptional items.
