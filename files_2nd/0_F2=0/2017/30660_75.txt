The Company established an EBT in 2011 in connection with the operation of the Companys share plans.
The Company funded the EBT by means of a cash loan and is therefore considered to be the sponsoring entity.
The EBT purchased shares in the Company using the cash loan which is accounted for as a purchase of own shares by the Company.
