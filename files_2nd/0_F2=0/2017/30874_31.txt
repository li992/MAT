Dividends The Board intends to recommend 25% of the Companys profit for the financial year to be distributed to the Companys shareholders as a dividend, which represents GEL 1.64 per share, payable in British Pounds Sterling at an official exchange rate of the National Bank of Georgia for 24 May 2018.
If approved, the final dividend will be paid on 22 June 2018 to shareholders on the Register of Members at the close of business in the UK ie 6pm London time on 18 May 2018.
