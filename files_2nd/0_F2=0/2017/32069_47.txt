Sports Direct International plc Annual Report 2017 OTHER BALANCE SHEET RESERVES The foreign currency translation reserve is used to record exchange differences arising from the translation of the Financial Statements of foreign subsidiaries and associates.
The own shares and treasury shares reserve represent the cost of shares in Sports Direct International plc purchased in the market and held by Sports Direct Employee Benefit Trust to satisfy options under the Groups share options scheme, see note 23.
The Company announced on 28 April 2017 that it had instructed Citigroup Global Markets Limited in relation to an irrevocable nondiscretionary share buyback programme to purchase the Companys shares during the closed period commencing on 30 April 2017 and ending on 20 July 2017.
In line with IAS32 the Company recognised the full redemption amount of 163.5m which is considered to be immaterially different to the present value at year end.
If the contract expires without full delivery, the amount of the financial liability attributable to the undelivered shares is reclassified to equity reversing the original recognition.
As at 18 July 2017, 94.1m of shares have been repurchased under the closed period share buyback programme.
