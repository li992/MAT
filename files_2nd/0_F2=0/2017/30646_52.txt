Overview Business review Financial review Governance Financial statements and other information Financial statements and other information Notes to the consolidated financial statements 153 20 Inventories and pre-publication costs Accounting policy Inventories and pre-publication costs are stated at the lower of cost, including appropriate attributable overhead, and estimated net realisable value.
Such costs typically comprise direct internal labour costs and externally commissioned editorial and other fees.
Pre-publication costs, representing costs incurred in the origination of content prior to publication, are expensed systematically reecting the expected sales prole over the estimated economic lives of the related products, generally up to five years.
Annual reviews are carried out to assess the recoverability of carrying amounts.
