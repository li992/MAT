15 Impairment of goodwill Goodwill acquired through business combinations has been allocated for impairment testing purposes to the groups of cash generating units CGUs listed below.

The remaining groups of CGUs are included in the balance of CGUs column.
The recoverable amount of all the CGUs is based on a value in use calculation using cash flow projections based on the latest one year budget forecast extrapolated for four future years by a growth rate applicable to each unit: an appropriate terminal value based on a perpetuity calculation using nil real growth is then added.
A discount rate of 11.1% is then applied to these projections.
