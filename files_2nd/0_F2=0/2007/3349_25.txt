Dividend Re-investment Plan DRIP The Company will not be operating the DRIP in respect of the Final Dividend for the year ended 31 December 2007.
All holders of mandates to participate in the DRIP will not be able to instruct the Company to use their dividend to buy shares in the Company on their behalf but will instead receive a cash dividend.
