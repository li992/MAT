Investments in joint ventures Principal joint ventures Unless otherwise stated, the Companys principal joint ventures all have share capital consisting solely of ordinary shares, which are indirectly held, and the country of incorporation or registration is also their principal place of operation.
They have the same year end date as the Company, unless otherwise stated, and have been included in the Consolidated Financial Statements.
Summarised financial information of joint ventures is disclosed in note 37.

As a result of the terms of this agreement, the Group considers that it does not have the power to exercise joint control and, consequently, has transferred its investment in Bharti Airtel Limited to Other Investments.
