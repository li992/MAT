Shareholder information Shareholder analysis At 28 February 2008, the number of registered shareholders was 5,003 and the number of Ordinary shares in issue was 777.6 million excluding treasury shares.
The Company operates a Level 1 American Depositary Receipt ADR programme with each ADR representing four Spirent Ordinary shares.
The ADRs trade on the US over-the-counter market under the symbol SPMYY and the CUSIP number is 84856M209.
The Bank of New York Mellon is the authorised depositary bank for the Companys ADR programme.
