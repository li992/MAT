Employee share schemes The Group operates a number of share plans for the benefit of employees.
Details of each plan are set out below.
In each table the price and number of shares for the comparative periods has been restated to reflect the sub-division of the Companys ordinary shares by way of the three for one split on 31 July 2006. i Sharesave Scheme An all-employee savings related plan is operated that enables employees, including executive directors, to invest up to a maximum of 250 per month for three or five years.
These savings can then be used to buy ordinary shares at a price set at a 20% discount to the market value at the start of the savings period at the third, fifth or seventh anniversary of the option being granted.
Options expire six months following the exercise date and, except for certain specific circumstances such as redundancy, lapse if the employee leaves the Group before the option exercise period commences.
