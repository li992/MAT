NOTES TO THE FINANCIAL STATEMENTS for the year ended 31st December 2007 3.
Discontinued Operations On 8th September 2006 the Group announced that it had reached an agreement with Alliant Insurance Services, Inc. for the sale of its US based Property and Casualty Insurance and Employee Benefits businesses.
The loss on sale before tax was 16.3 million and included the write off of goodwill and all related transaction costs.
It was reported as an exceptional item in discontinued operations.
