PROPERTY, PLANT AND EQUIPMENT continued On 31 December 2007, the Groups freehold properties and terminals were valued on the bases of either existing use value or market value.
The freehold properties in the UK were valued by Savills L&P Limited, Chartered Surveyors and Ryden LLP, acting as independent valuers, in accordance with the Royal Institute of Chartered Surveyors Appraisal and Valuation Manual Practice Statement 1 and 5.
Freehold properties and terminals in Canada were valued by American Appraisal Canada Inc, acting as independent valuers.
Freehold properties in the US were valued by Cushman & Wakefield Inc, acting as independent valuers.
Freehold property in the Netherlands was valued by Brantjes Makelaars, acting as independent valuers.
The Group previously undertook a valuation on 31 December 2002.
