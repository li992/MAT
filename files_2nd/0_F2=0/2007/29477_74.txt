The EEV result has been calculated on an after-tax basis and has been grossed up to a pre-tax level for presentation in the profit and loss account.
The corporation tax rate used for this grossing up is 26% for UK life and pensions business, 12.5% for Irish life and pensions business and 28% for unit trust business.
The tax rates reflect the change in the rate of corporation tax from 30% to 28% with effect from 1 April 2008, as included within the 2007 Finance Act.
