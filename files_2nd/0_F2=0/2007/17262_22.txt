The gearing ratio is the ratio of net borrowings, excluding construction loan borrowings, to shareholders funds.
Return on gross capital employed is the ratio of trading profit before loss on disposal of operations and the amortisation and impairment of goodwill and acquired intangibles to the aggregate of average shareholders funds, minority interests, net debt and cumulative goodwill written off.
