Summary combined financial information in euros 140 Notes to the summary combined financial information in euros 2 Discontinued operations Following announcement in February 2007 of the planned sale of Harcourt Education, the division is presented as a discontinued operation.
On 4 May 2007 the sale of the Harcourt Assessment and Harcourt Education International businesses for $950m was announced, and on 16 July 2007 the sale of the Harcourt US Schools Education businesses for $4.0bn was announced.
All disposals had completed by 31 December 2007, with the exception of Harcourt Assessment and certain Harcourt International businesses, the disposal of which completed on 30 January 2008.
Those businesses are presented in the balance sheet as assets held for sale.
