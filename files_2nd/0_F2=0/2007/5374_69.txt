9 Retirement benefits The Company operates one defined contribution scheme and one defined benefit scheme.
The assets of the schemes are held by independent custodians and are kept entirely separate from the assets of the Company.
No loans have been made by any scheme to the Company or any of its subsidiaries and no shareholdings of the schemes have been used as security for any loans to the Company or any of its subsidiaries.

b Defined benefit plan The Company operates a defined benefit scheme for qualifying employees in the UK, the Emap Earnings Related Pension Plan EERPP.
Under the scheme rules, participants are entitled to retirement benefits varying between 0% and 67% of final salary on the attainment of retirement age.
The scheme is closed to new participants.
