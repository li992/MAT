On 23 December 2005, the Group increased its shareholding from 50% to 75% for a consideration of 1.5 million.
Following the increase in shareholding, Ayres is fully consolidated into the Groups results.
The Group previously accounted for its interest in Ayres using the equity method.
The financial information of the Groups investment in Ayres up to the date of full consolidation consisted of 4.3 million and 0.6 million in respect of revenue and loss after tax, respectively.
