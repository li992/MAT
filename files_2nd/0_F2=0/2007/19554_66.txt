The key assumptions used in the calculation are as follows: Risk free rate 3.6% pa 5.0% pa depending on grant date and expected life Volatility 28% pa 37% pa depending on grant date Employee turnover 2.5% pa for DSBP and Sharesave and zero for ESOS Early exercise 50% of employees exercise early when options and awards are 20% in the money Performance criteria All vest after three years only relevant for ESOS The expected volatility is based on historical volatility over the last five years.
The risk free rate is the yield on a zero coupon UK government bonds at each grant date, with term based on the expected life of the option or award.
