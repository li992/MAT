EARNINGS PER ORDINARY SHARE Basic and diluted earnings per share are calculated by dividing the profit for the year by the weighted average number of ordinary shares at the end of the year and the weighted average number of ordinary shares diluted at the end of the year, respectively.
In order to better demonstrate the performance of the Group, an adjusted earnings per share calculation has been presented which adds back items typically adjusted for by users of the accounts, using adjusted profit for the year.
