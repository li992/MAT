Financial risk management continued Credit risk continued The credit risk relating to cash, deposits and derivative financial instruments is actively managed centrally by Liberty International PLC, the ultimate parent.
Relationships are maintained with a number of tier one institutional counterparties, ensuring compliance with Liberty International PLC Group policy relating to limits on the credit ratings of counterparties between BBB and AAA.
Excessive credit risk is avoiding through adhering to authorised limits for all counterparties.
