89 Great Portland Estates Annual Report 2009 Annual review Governance Financials Notes forming part of the Company financial statements i Accounting policies Accounting convention The Company financial statements are prepared under UK GAAP and the historical cost convention as modified by the revaluation of investments in subsidiary undertakings.
The Company has prepared its financial statements on a going concern basis, see page 48.
Subsidiary undertakings and joint ventures Shares in subsidiary undertakings are valued at amounts equal to their original cost and any subsequent movement in the revaluation reserve of those subsidiaries, thus reflecting in the Companys balance sheet the surplus arising from the revaluation and the sale of investments and investment properties of those subsidiaries.
