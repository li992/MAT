EXTRACTING VALUE Kazakhmys is a leading natural resources group with our main operations in Kazakhstan and the surrounding countries of Central Asia.
Our primary operations are in copper, with significant interests in zinc, silver, gold, power generation and petroleum.
Our Focus is to combine efficient, safe and responsible operations with the exceptional long-term growth opportunities which are available in the region building sustainable value for the benefit of all of our stakeholders and the communities in which we operate.
