NOTES For the 52 weeks ended 3 October 2009 1 ACCOUNTING POLICIES Basis of preparation These financial statements for the 52 weeks ended 3 October 2009 2008: 53 weeks ended 4 October 2008 have been prepared in accordance with IFRS and International Financial Reporting Interpretations Committee IFRIC and Standing Interpretations Committee SIC interpretations adopted by the European Union and with those parts of the Companies Act 2006 applicable to companies reporting under IFRS.
The financial statements have been prepared under the historical cost convention as modified by the revaluation of certain items, principally land and buildings, derivative financial instruments and share-based payments.
