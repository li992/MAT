30. Business combinations Belside Limited On 2 October 2009 the company acquired 100% of the issued share capital of Belside Limited from Capvestco Limited, a fellow subsidiary undertaking of Liberty International PLC, for a consideration of 57.2 million.
The principal activity of Belside Limited is the ownership, management and development of the Mall, the Retail Park, the former BT Building, at Cribbs Causeway, Bristol.
The business contributed net revenues of 1.9 million, and a net profit of 10.6 million after recognising a surplus on revaluation of investment and development property of 8.7 million.
Had the acquisition occurred on 1 January 2009 the Group net revenue would have been 3.2 million higher, and the Group net loss would have been 28.8 million higher, after charging a deficit on revaluation of investment and development property of 32.0 million.
