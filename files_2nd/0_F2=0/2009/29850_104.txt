c Fair value of financial instruments The International Accounting Standards Board IASB has made amendments to IFRS 7 Financial Instruments: Disclosures which is applicable for annual periods beginning on or after 1 January 2009.
These amendments introduce a fair value hierarchy in its required disclosures.
The three-tier hierarchy of inputs is summarised in the three broad Levels listed below.
Level 1 quoted prices in active markets for identical investments.
Level 2 other significant observable inputs including quoted prices for similar investments, interest rates, prepayment, credit risk, etc.
Level 3 significant unobservable inputs including the Funds own assumptions in determining the fair value of investments.
