These letters of credit, covering the full period of the finance leases, are renewable between the financial institutions and South West Water Limited at five-yearly intervals, the next being March 2011.
During 2007 the period for repayment of these leases was extended with an agreement to deposit with the lessor group amounts equal to the difference between the original and revised payments due.
The accumulated deposits, 16.7m at 31 March 2009 2008 10.1m, are being held to settle the lease liability over the period from the end of the original lease term.
