Segmental information For both management and financial reporting purposes the Group is organised into three operating businesses HMV UK & Ireland, HMV International, comprising HMV Canada, HMV Hong Kong and HMV Singapore, and Waterstones.

Waterstones is the book retailing division of HMV Group, primarily trading under the Waterstones brand.
