Excess management expenses relating to the private equity funds portfolio are available to set against any taxable income of the Company.
These excess management expenses are included within the investments in private equity funds in the balance sheet of the Company and are not reflected in the Companys revenue account.
If in a future year in relation to the private equity fund investments income exceeds expenses, the taxation charge to the Companys revenue account will include tax on this excess with a suitable note by way of explanation.
