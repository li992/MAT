Approximately 40% of the total dividend in respect of any fi financial year is expected to be paid as an interim dividend and approximately 60% as a fi final dividend.
The payment of any future dividends, subject to shareholder approval, will depend upon Diageos earnings, fi financial condition and such other factors as the board deems relevant.
Proposed dividends are not considered to be a liability until they are approved by the board for the interim dividend and by the shareholders at the annual general meeting for the fi final dividend.
