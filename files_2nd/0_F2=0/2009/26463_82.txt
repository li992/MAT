35. BUSINESS COMBINATIONS On 1 October 2007, the Group acquired 99.92 per cent of Borsa Italiana S. p. A. the company responsible for the organisation and management of the securities market in Italy.
The total consideration of 1,321.5m, including 13.8m costs, was financed by the issue of 79.4m London Stock Exchange Group plc shares with a total market value of 1,307.7m on the date of acquisition, together with 13.8m costs paid in cash.
Subsequently the Group acquired a further 0.04 per cent, bringing its total shareholding to 99.96 per cent.
