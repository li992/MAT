Unauthorised brokers boiler room scams Shareholders are advised to be very wary of any unsolicited advice, offers to buy shares at a discount or offers of free company reports.
These are typically from overseas based brokers who target UK shareholders offering to sell them what often turn out to be worthless or high risk shares in US or UK investments.
More detailed information can be found on the FSA website, at www.
If you receive unsolicited investment advice: Make sure you get the correct name of the person and organisation.
Check that they are properly authorised by the FSA before getting involved.
The FSA also maintains a list of unauthorised overseas firms who are targeting, or have targeted, UK investors.
