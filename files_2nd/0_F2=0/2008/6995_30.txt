The goodwill arose on the acquisition of Halfords Holdings Limited by the Company on 31 August 2002.
The goodwill relates to a portfolio of sites, which have been allocated to groups of cash-generating units on a regional basis within the UK according to the level at which management monitors that goodwill.
The recoverable amount of goodwill is determined based on value-in-use calculations.
These calculations use cash flow projections based on financial budgets approved by management covering a five-year period with growth no higher than past experience and after consideration of all available information.
