OPERATING LEASE ARRANGEMENTS The Group leases a number of trading properties under operating leases.
The leases are typically 25 years in duration, although some have lessee only break clauses of between 10 and 15 years.
Lease payments are reviewed every five years and increases applied in line with market rates.
The Group also leases certain items of plant and equipment.
The Company has no operating lease arrangements.
