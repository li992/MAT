Our Business The Group exists to help customers save time and money.
Product providers offer customers a wide range of insurance, money, travel and home services products to choose from.
As the UKs leading price comparison website, the Group provides customers with a free online service to compare products and to find the one most suited to their needs.
The Group provides its price comparison services to customers via two market leading brands, moneysupermarket.
