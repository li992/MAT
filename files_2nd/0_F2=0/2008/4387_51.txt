In July 2007, the Group issued 141,500,000 of Guaranteed convertible bonds due in 2014.
Convertible bonds The bonds have a conversion premium of 50%, representing a conversion price of 16.45 per Dana share, with a coupon of 2.9% payable semi-annually.
The bonds have a seven year term and include an investor put on the fifth anniversary of the issue date.
At the initial conversion price of 16.45 per share, there are 8,601,824 ordinary shares of the Company underlying the bonds.
