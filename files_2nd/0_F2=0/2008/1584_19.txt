Segmental analysis Whilst the group currently operates under a number of different divisions, the groups core technology, types of revenue and associated costs and returns are comparable.
Each of these divisions is founded on the groups unique Intelligent Data Operating Layer, the groups core infrastructure for automating the handling of all forms of unstructured information.
As a result, the group maintains only one reportable business segment, as is reflected in the reporting in this annual report.
The groups operations are located primarily in the United Kingdom, the US and Canada.
The company also has a significant presence in a number of other European countries as well as China, Japan, Singapore and Australia.
