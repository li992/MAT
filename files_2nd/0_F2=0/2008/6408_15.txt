02 DIRECTORS & GOVERNANCE 33 THE GAME GROUP PLC | ANNUAL REPORT AND ACCOUNTS 2007-2008 Charitable and political donations The Group made charitable donations of over 30,000 to the childrens charity NCH.
The Group does not make donations to any political party.
Creditor payment policy The Groups policy for the year to 31 January 2008, for all suppliers, was to agree terms of payment when establishing the terms of each business transaction and to abide by the agreed terms of payment.
The Group had an average 42 days purchases included in trade creditors at the year end.
The Company had no trade creditors.
Financial instruments The main risks arising from the Groups financial instruments are interest rate risk, liquidity risk, foreign currency risk and credit risk.
The Board reviews and agrees policies for managing each of these risks.
Further details on the Groups financial instruments can be found in the Statement of Accounting Policies and notes 16 and 17 to the financial statements.
Corporate Social Responsibility The Group recognises that to remain successful over the longer term the business must take an open, honest and responsible approach towards all of its key stakeholders employees, customers, investors and the wider community.
The full report is set out on pages 23 to 27.
Principal risks and uncertainties The principal risks and uncertainties facing the Group are detailed in note 27 to the financial statements.
Share capital Details of changes in share capital during the year ended 31 January 2008 are set out in note 20 to the financial statements.
As at 31 January 2008 the Company had an unexpired authority to repurchase further shares up to a maximum of 33,000,000 shares.
During the year no shares were purchased in the market for cancellation.
