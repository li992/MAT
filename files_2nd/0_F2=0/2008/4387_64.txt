Events since the balance On 16 February 2009, the Group announced that it had entered into an agreement to acquire the outstanding share capital of Bow Valley Energy Ltd, a Canadian listed, but sheet date UK focused, upstream company for a cash consideration of CAN $0.50 per share subject to shareholder and regulatory approvals.
In addition to the equity consideration of approximately USD35 million equivalent, the Group will also repay Bow Valleys bank debt at the completion of the transaction.
At 31 December 2008, Bow Valley had an estimated USD142 million of net bank debt.
To facilitate the transaction, and as a broader corporate refinancing initiative, the Company also agreed a new three-year USD400 million revolving credit facility with the Bank of Scotland.
This replaces the existing facility with ABN AMRO Bank N. V. which has been cancelled.
