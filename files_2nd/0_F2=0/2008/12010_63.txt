 The Group operates a number of pension schemes including a defined contribution section within the main scheme.
The assets of the Groups pension schemes are held in separate trustee administered funds.
The trustees of the funds are required to act in the best interest of the funds beneficiaries.
The appointment of schemes trustees is determined by the schemes trust documentation.
The Group has a policy that one-half of all trustees, other than the Chairman, are nominated by members of the schemes, including current pensioners.
