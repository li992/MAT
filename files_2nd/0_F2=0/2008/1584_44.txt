Post-Balance Sheet Events On 22 January 2009 the company announced that it had entered into a definitive agreement to acquire Interwoven, Inc. a global leader in content management software, for aggregate consideration of approximately $775 million assuming exercise of all vested in-the-money Interwoven share options, funded by an underwritten placing of ordinary shares, a new revolving credit facility from Barclays and a portion of Interwoven and Autonomys cash reserves.
On the same date the company successfully completed the placing of 21,560,620 new ordinary shares at a placing price of 10.33 per share, raising gross proceeds of 222,721,205.
