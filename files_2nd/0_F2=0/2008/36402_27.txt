Great Portland Estates Annual Report 2008 79 Notes forming part of the Company financial statements i Accounting policies Accounting convention The Company financial statements are prepared under UK GAAP and the historical cost convention as modified by the revaluation of investments in subsidiary undertakings.
Subsidiary undertakings and joint ventures Shares in subsidiary undertakings are valued at amounts equal to their original cost and any subsequent movement in the revaluation reserve of those subsidiaries, thus reflecting in the Companys balance sheet the surplus arising from the revaluation and the sale of investments and investment properties of those subsidiaries.
