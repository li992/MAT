SHARE CAPITAL AND WARRANTS continued ii Exercise of Warrants The Warrants are exercisable on 15 June in each of the years 2006 to 2010 inclusive.
Each Warrant carries the right to subscribe for one Ordinary Share in cash at a subscription price of 96 pence.
On 16 June 2008 the holders of 659,168 Warrants subscribed in cash for Ordinary Shares on a one Warrant for one Ordinary Share basis and at a subscription price of 96p per Ordinary Share.
As a result 659,168 Ordinary Shares were issued.
In aggregate, net proceeds of 632,801 were received for these Ordinary Shares.
The new Ordinary Shares were admitted to trading on 23 June 2008.
At the year end 19,104,153 Warrants remained in issue.
