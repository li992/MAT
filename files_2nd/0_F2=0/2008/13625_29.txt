Analysis of revenue, profit and net assets The primary format used for segmental reporting is by business segment as this reflects the internal management structure and reporting of the Group.
Segment results, assets and liabilities include items directly attributable to a segment as well as those that can be allocated on a reasonable basis.
Unallocated expenses comprise corporate expenses and unallocated assets and liabilities comprise cash, borrowings, tax assets and liabilities respectively.
Intragroup trading is determined on an arms length basis.
Business segments The Group comprises the following business segments: Controls the design, manufacture and sale of electric valve actuators Fluid Systems the design, manufacture and sale of heavy duty pneumatic and hydraulic valve actuators Gears the design, manufacture and sale of gearboxes, adaption and ancillaries for the valve industry Geographic segments Rotork has a worldwide presence in all three business segments through its subsidiary selling offices and through an agency network.
