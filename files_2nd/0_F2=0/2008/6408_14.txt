32 THE GAME GROUP PLC | ANNUAL REPORT AND ACCOUNTS 2007-2008 REPORT OF THE DIRECTORS for the year ended 31 January 2008 The Directors have pleasure in presenting their Report on the affairs of the Group, together with the audited financial statements, for the year ended 31 January 2008.
Principal activities The Group operates retail outlets which specialise in the retailing of computer software, video games and related products.
The Parent Company The GAME Group plc is an investment holding company.
Business review As at 31 January 2008 the Group operated in four European territories and in Australia, with 649 stores throughout the United Kingdom and the Republic of Ireland, 208 in Iberia, 170 in France, 62 in Scandinavia and 51 in Australia, as well as operating eCommerce sites.
A more detailed review of the business for the year ended 31 January 2008 and future developments is set out in the Chairmans Statement on pages 8 to 10, the Chief Executives Review on pages 11 to 13 and the Business Review on pages 16 to 22.
Results and dividends The consolidated profit after taxation was 47.2 million 2007: 21.1 million, on turnover of 1,491.9 million 2007: 801.3 million.
This profit represents basic earnings per share of 13.79p 2007: 6.25p.
The Directors recommend a final dividend of 2.97p 2007: 1.66p per share.
This, when taken with the interim dividend of 1.43p 2007: 1.27p per share, gives a total dividend of 4.40p 2007: 2.93p per share for the year ended 31 January 2008.
