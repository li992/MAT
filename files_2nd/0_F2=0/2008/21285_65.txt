The Bankers Investment Trust PLC Report & Financial Statements 2008 55 Notes to the Financial Statements continued 20 Capital redemption and other capital reserves continued Under the terms of the Companys articles of association, sums standing to the credit of capital reserves are distributable only by way of redemption or purchase of any of the Companys own shares, for so long as the Company carries on business as an investment company.
Company law states that investment companies may only distribute accumulated realised profits.
This technical interpretation of the meaning of distributable reserves would, as a consequence, give rise at 31 October 2008 to capital reserves available for distribution other than by way of dividend in excess of 300 million after adjusting unrealised capital losses on quoted investments of 526,000.
