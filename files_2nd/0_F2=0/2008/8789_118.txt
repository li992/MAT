Business combinations Acquisition of Yuzhgazenergie LLC On 22nd November 2007, the Group acquired 100% of the share capital of Yuzhgazenerie LLC, an oil and gas exploration and development company based in Russia.
Yuzhgazenergie LLC was owned by Mostotal Investments Limited Mostotal.
Mostotal and Glengary Overseas Limited Glengary were controlled by Mr Alexander Zhukov.
Glengary, held a 25.15% shareholding in JKX Oil and Gas plc at the date of acquisition.
As a related party transaction the acquisition was approved by the shareholders of JKX Oil and Gas plc at an Extraordinary General Meeting.
Details of net assets acquired and goodwill are as follows: 2007 $000 Purchase consideration: Cash paid 41,610 Discounted deferred consideration 9,044 Direct costs relating to the acquisition 2,974 Total purchase consideration 53,628 Fair value of net assets acquired 50,912 Goodwill on acquisition 2,716 The goodwill arises after the application of IAS 12 Income taxes and is attributable principally to expanded growth opportunities in Russia.
During the year $5.119m was paid to Mostotal as part of the deferred consideration.
At 31st December 2008 the remaining deferred consideration was $4.545m undiscounted: $5.000m payable on 22nd November 2009.
There has been no change to the provisional fair value during the year.
