E-communications for shareholders SVG Capital would like to encourage shareholders to receive shareholder documents electronically, via our website or by email notification instead of hard copy format.
This is a faster and more environmentally friendly way of receiving shareholder documents.
The online Shareview service from our registrar, Equiniti, provides all the information required regarding your shares.
Its features include: The option to receive shareholder communications electronically instead of by post Direct access to data held for you on the share register including recent share movements and dividend details The ability to change your address or dividend payment instructions online To receive shareholder communications electronically in future, including all reports and notices of meetings, you just need the shareholder reference printed on your proxy form or dividend notices, and knowledge of your registered address.
