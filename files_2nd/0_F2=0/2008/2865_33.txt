The Capita Group Plc 89 5 Segmental information The Groups operations are organised and managed separately according to the nature of the services provided, with each segment representing a strategic business unit offering a different package of related services across the Groups markets.
A description of the service provision for each segment can be found in the business review on pages 54-60.
Before eliminating sales between business units on consolidation, the Group accounts for sales between business units as if they were to a third party at market rates.
