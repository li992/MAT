As part of this transaction Investec plc has realised a gain of 61.6 million on the disposal of a non-controlling interest in its subsidiary, Investec Asset Management Limited, which is refl ected in equity together with an increase in non-controlling interests of 11.0 million.
Investec Limited has realised a gain of 82.2 million on the disposal of a non-controlling interest in its subsidiary, Investec Asset Management Holdings Pty Ltd, which is refl ected in equity together with an increase in non-controlling interests of 12.1 million.
The group has funded 44.2 million of this acquisition, resulting in net proceeds of 122.7 million.
