Income tax expense Accounting policy Income tax on the profit for the year comprises current and deferred tax.
Income tax is recognized in the income statement except to the extent that it relates to items recognized in other comprehensive income or directly in equity.
In this case the tax is also recognized in other comprehensive income or directly in equity, respectively.
Current tax is the expected tax payable on the taxable income for the year, using tax rates enacted, or substantively enacted, at the balance sheet date, and any adjustment to tax payable in respect of previous years.
