Merger relief reserve At 1 April 2013 the merger relief reserve comprised US$3,395 million in respect of the excess of value attributed to the shares issued as consideration for Miller Brewing Company over the nominal value of those shares and US$1,191 million relating to the merger relief arising on the issue of SABMiller plc ordinary shares for the buyout of non-controlling interests in the groups Polish business.
In the year ended 31 March 2014 the group transferred US$265 million of the reserve relating to the Polish business to retained earnings upon realisation of qualifying consideration.
