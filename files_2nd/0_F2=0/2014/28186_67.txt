The Subscription shares were issued as a bonus issue to the Ordinary shareholders on 14 February 2011 on the basis of one Subscription share for every five Ordinary shares.
Each Subscription share conferred the right but not the obligation to subscribe for one Ordinary share on the last business day of each month from 31 March 2011 until 31 March 2014 when the rights under the Subscription shares lapsed.
During the year, holders of 3,404,417 Subscription shares exercised their right to convert those shares into Ordinary shares at a price of 478.0 pence per share.
Following the subsequent appointment of a trustee, pursuant to paragraph 8 h of Part 4 of the Particulars and Procedures in respect of the Subscription shares set out in the prospectus of the Company dated 18 January 2011, the remaining 21,370,043 Subscription shares were converted to deferred shares and cancelled 2.
An additional 700,000 ordinary shares were issued during the year for a total consideration of 3,296,000 2013: nil.
