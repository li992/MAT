The Board of the Company has considered the principles and recommendations of the AIC Code by reference to the AIC Corporate Governance Guide for Investment Companies the AIC Guide.
The AIC Code, as explained by the AIC Guide addresses all the principles set out in the UK Corporate Governance Code, as well as setting out additional principles and recommendations on issues that are of specific relevance to an investment company.
The Board considers that reporting against the principles and recommendations of the AIC Code, and reference to the AIC Guide which incorporates the UK Corporate Governance Code, will provide better information to shareholders.
The Company is also required to comply with the GFSC Code.
As the Company reports under the AIC Code it is deemed to meet the requirements of the GFSC Code.
The Board has undertaken to evaluate its corporate governance compliance on an on-going basis.
