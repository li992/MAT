The voucher entitles the recipient or their immediate family to a 25% discount against most purchases at any one time of full price merchandise in NEXT Retail stores.
It cannot be used in conjunction with any other discount voucher or offer, nor can it be used for the purchase of gift cards, Sale merchandise, electrical goods, non-NEXT branded goods or at any branch of NEXT Clearance or purchases from NEXT Directory.
The voucher has no monetary purchase limit and expires on 31 October of the same year.
Shareholders holding shares in nominee or PEP ISA accounts are also eligible, but must request the voucher through their nominee or PEP ISA account manager who should email alyson wenlock@next.
