Capital risk management The Company manages its capital to ensure that it is able to continue as a going concern while maximising the return to stakeholders through the appropriate balance of debt and equity.
The capital structure of the Company consists of debt, which includes the borrowings disclosed in note 20, cash and cash equivalents and equity comprising issued capital, reserves and retained earnings as disclosed in notes 25, 26, 27, 30 and 53 and the Company Statement of Changes in Equity.
