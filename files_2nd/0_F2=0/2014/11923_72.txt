Retirement benefit and other post-retirement obligations Background The Group operates a number of defined benefit and defined contribution retirement plans throughout the world.
The largest plan is the Pearson Group Pension Plan UK Group plan in the UK, which is sectionalised to provide both defined benefit and defined contribution pension benefits.
The defined benefit section was closed to new members from 1 November 2006.
The defined contribution section, opened in 2003, is open to new and existing employees.
Finally, there is a separate section within the UK Group plan set up for auto-enrolment.
The defined benefit section of the UK Group plan is a final salary pension plan which provides benefits to members in the form of a guaranteed level of pension payable for life.
The level of benefits depends on the length of service and final pensionable pay.
The UK Group plan is funded with benefit payments from trustee administered funds.
The UK Group plan is administered inaccordance with the Trust Deed and Rules in the interests of its beneficiaries by Pearson Group Pension TrusteeLimited.
