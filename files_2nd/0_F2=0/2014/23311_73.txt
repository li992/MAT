Related Party Transactions and Transactions with the Manager A related party is a company or individual who has direct or indirect control or who has significant influence over the Company.
Under accounting standards, the Manager is not a related party but disclosures are made in accordance with industry practice.
Under UK GAAP, the Company has identified the Directors as related parties.
The Directors remuneration and interests have been disclosed on pages 33 to 35 with additional disclosure in note 4.
No other related parties have been identified.
Invesco Asset Management Limited IAML, a wholly owned subsidiary of Invesco Limited, acts as Manager to the Company.
Details of the investment management agreement are disclosed in the Directors Report and management fees payable to IAML are shown in note 3.
