6 Exceptional items Exceptional items are items of income and expenditure that, in the judgement of management, should be disclosed separately on the basis that they are material, either by their nature or their size, to an understanding of the financial performance and significantly distort the comparability of financial performance between accounting periods.
Items of income or expense that are considered by management for designation as exceptional, include such items as major restructuring and significant impairments.
