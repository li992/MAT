7 Sale of PruHealth and PruProtect businesses On 10 November 2014, the Prudential Assurance Company Limited announced an agreement to sell its 25 per cent equity stake in the PruHealth and PruProtect businesses to Discovery Group Europe Limited.
The sale was completed on 14 November 2014.
This transaction gave rise to a gain on disposal of 44 million.
8 Held for sale Japan life business On 5 February 2015, the Group announced that it had completed the sale of its closed book life insurance business in Japan, PCA Life Insurance Company Limited to SBI Holdings, Inc. following regulatory approvals.
The loss of Japan life business in the 2013 results includes the reduction in EEV carrying value to reflect the completion of sale.
