Assets held for sale As part of the Strategic Review certain assets and liabilities have been designated as non-core and are held for sale.
As at 31 December 2014 the following businesses have been disclosed as held for sale: National Physical Laboratory, Great Southern Rail, the UK environmental and leisure businesses, the offshore BPO business and the majority of the UK private BPO business.
While a significant portion of the Global Services CGU has been transferred to held for sale, as it does not represent the whole of a separate line of business, it is not appropriate to treat as a discontinued operation.
