The Group hedging strategy is to hold debt in proportion to the group profit and cash flow which are mainly EUR and USD.
As a result the group has swapped a proportion of the bond issue into USD debt which has increased effective interest rate.
On 22 November 2017 the Group issued a new 400m bond at a coupon of 0.95% under its EMTN Programme.
Part of the proceeds were swapped into USD.
The rate on the USD swaps was floating for the first year at 3 month LIBOR 1.08% and fixed at 3.38% from November 2018 until maturity.
The Group considers the fair value of other current liabilities to be equal to the carrying value.
