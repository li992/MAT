TRADE AND OTHER PAYABLES Overview Trade and other payables include amounts owed to suppliers, tax authorities and other parties that are typically due to be settled within 12 months.
The total also includes contract liabilities, which represents monies received from customers but for which we have not yet delivered the associated service.
These amounts are recognised as revenue when the service is provided.
Other payables includes deferred consideration in respect of business combinations, dividends payable to non-controlling interests and employee-related payables.
