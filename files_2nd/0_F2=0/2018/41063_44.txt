In line with IAS 39 Financial Instruments: Recognition and Measurement, the asset was remeasured to fair value before the sale completed, resulting in a 14m increase in the book value of the investment.
On disposal the 14m revaluation gain was recognised in the income statement net of 1m of transaction costs.
This profit is one-off in nature and was excluded from reported underlying earnings for the 52 weeks ended 29 January 2017 see note 1.4.
Following the transaction the undrawn loan facility provided to Fresh Direct Inc ceased.
