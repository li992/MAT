During the year, the Company continued to reduce its level of debt, through the partial early settlement of the 2026 sterling bonds, and the 2020 euro bond.
Borrowings are denominated in sterling and euros, and bear fixed interest rates.
The Group has a syndicated committed revolving credit facility of 1.35bn with a maturity date of September 2021.
The revolving credit facility incurs commitment fees and drawdowns bear interest at floating interest rates at a spread above LIBOR.
There were no borrowings under the revolving credit facility at the balance sheet date.
Therefore the Group had 1.35bn of undrawn committed borrowing facilities available 2017: 1.35bn.
In the event of default of covenants on the bank facility, the principal amounts and any interest accrued are repayable on demand.
