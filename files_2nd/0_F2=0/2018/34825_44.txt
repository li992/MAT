Jointly Controlled Operations The Group holds a 46.7% interest in a joint arrangement Digital Cinema Implementation Partners DCIP.

DCIPs principal place of business is the United States.
The Group recognises its direct right to the assets, liabilities, revenues and expenses of DCIP under the appropriate headings.
