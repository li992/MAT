Dividend payments to shareholders are split between PropertyIncome Distributions PIDs and non PIDs.
For most shareholders, PIDs will be paid after deducting withholding tax at the basic rate.
However, certain categories of shareholder are entitled to receive PIDs without withholding tax, principally UK resident companies, UK public bodies, UKpension funds and managers of ISAs, PEPs and Child Trust Funds.
There is a form on the Companys website for shareholders to certify that they qualify to receive PIDs without withholding tax.
