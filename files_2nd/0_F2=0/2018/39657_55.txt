FINANCIAL STATEMENTS 13.4 Borrowings Accounting policy Interest-bearing borrowings are recognised initially at fair value less attributable issue costs, which are amortised over the period of the borrowings.
Subsequent to initial recognition, interest-bearing borrowings are stated at amortised cost with any difference between cost and redemption value being recognised in the income statement over the period of borrowing on an effective interest basis.
