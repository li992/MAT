FTIS as Alternative Investment Fund Manager AIFM.
The contract between the Company and FTIS, its Alternative Investment Fund Manager and provider of Secretarial and Administration Services, may be terminated at any date by either party giving one years notice of termination.
The AIFM fee is paid monthly and based on the monthly trading total net assets of the Company.
From 1 July 2017, the AIFM fee was reduced from 1.1% of net assets per annum to 1% of net assets up to 2 billion and 0.85% of net assets above that level.
With effect from 1 April 2017, 70% of the annual AIFM fee has been allocated to the capital account.
For the year ended 31 March 2017, 100% of the annual AIFM fee was allocated to revenue.
