Subsequent events On 18 January 2019, the Company announced a dividend of 19.1 million, equivalent to 1.69 pence per share with respect to the quarter ended 31 December 2018, bringing the total dividend declared with respect to the year to 31 December 2018 to 6.76 pence per share.
