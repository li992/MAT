Hedging and translation reserve The hedging and translation reserve comprises the effective portion of the cumulative net change in the fair value of cash flow and foreign currency hedging instruments, as well as all foreign exchange differences arising from the translation of the financial statements of foreign operations.
The foreign exchange differences also include the translation of the liabilities that hedge the Companys net investment in a foreign subsidiary.
Revaluation reserve The revaluation reserve relates to owner-occupied properties and investments in joint ventures and funds.
Merger reserve This comprises the premium on the share placing in March 2013.
No share premium is recorded in the Companys financial statements, through the operation of the merger relief provisions of the Companies Act 2006.
