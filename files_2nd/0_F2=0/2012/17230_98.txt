Accordingly, the Groups profits for this accounting period are taxed at an effective rate of 26.3% and will be taxed at 26% in the future.
The impact of this change in tax rate is a credit of 42m to the income statement.
On 23 March 2011, it was announced that the rate of corporation tax will be reducing from 28% to 23% over a four year period.
The first reduction to 26% is applicable for the period ending 29 January 2012.
A further 1% reduction per year will bring the rate down to 23% by 2015.
At 29 January 2012, a rate of 25% had been substantively enacted and has been used in calculating the Groups deferred tax liability.
