Shares used to satisfy the groups obligations under the employee share plans can be newly issued shares, treasury shares or shares purchased on the open market by the employee share trusts.
Where shares held by employee share trusts have been allocated to employee share plan participants, they may exercise their voting rights.
Where shares are held by employee share trusts and have not been allocated to participants, the trustee abstains from voting.
