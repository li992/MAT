The March 2012 Budget announced that the standard rate of corporation tax would now change from 26% to 24% with effect from 1 April 2012.
Accordingly, the Groups losses for the current period are taxed at an effective rate of 25%.
The Budget also announced a further reduction in the standard rate of corporation tax from 24% to 22% phased in over two years at 1% per annum from April 2013.
The change from 24% to 23% with effect from 1 April 2013 was enacted in the Finance Act 2012.
An exceptional deferred tax credit of 2.1 million has been recognised in the current period in respect of this.
