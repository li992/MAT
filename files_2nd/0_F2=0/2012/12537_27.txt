During the year, the group undertook a private placement of senior loan notes of US$202.0 million and 25.0 million.
At the year-end date, the groups principal credit facilities comprised a US$300.0 million term loan, a US$695.0 million revolving credit facility and a 216.0 million US$351.1 million letter of credit facility, all maturing in 2015.
In addition, in March 2012, group also entered into a US$350.0 million revolving credit facility maturing in 2017.
Senior loan notes have various maturities starting from 2018 to 2024.
Convertible bonds exchange offer In October 2012, the group invited holders of the outstanding US$250.0 million guaranteed convertible bonds old bonds to exchange their existing bonds due 2014 for guaranteed convertible bonds due 2018 new bonds.
The new bonds have a conversion price of US$7.00 per share and a coupon of 2.5 per cent.
This compared with the old bonds which had a conversion price of US$6.69 per share and a coupon of 2.875 per cent.
Bond holders representing 98.13 per cent of the total aggregate principal amount of the US$250.0 million approximately US$245.3 million elected to exchange their old bonds for the new bonds.
The remainder of the old bonds were settled in cash US$4.6 million or company shares US$0.1 million at par.
