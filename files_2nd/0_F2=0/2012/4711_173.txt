Employees continued statements 117 The investment strategy for the groups funded post employment plans is decided locally by the trustee of the plan and or Diageo, as Auditors report in appropriate, and takes account of the relevant statutory requirements.
The objective for the investment strategy is to achieve a target respect of the Group rate of return in excess of the movement on the liabilities, while taking an acceptable amount of investment risk relative to the liabilities.
118 This objective is implemented by using specific allocations to a variety of asset classes that are expected over the long term to deliver Consolidated income statement the target rate of return.
Most investment strategies have significant allocations to equities, with the intention that this will result in the ongoing cost to the group of the post employment plans being lower over the long term, within acceptable boundaries of risk.
