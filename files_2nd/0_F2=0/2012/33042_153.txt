Total shareholder return TSR is the measure of the returns that a company has generated for its shareholders, reecting share price movements and assuming reinvestment of dividends.
BTs TSR for the 2012 financial year was positive 27.4%, compared with the FTSE100 TSR which was positive 1.2% and the FTSEurorst 300 Telco Index TSR which was negative 5.8%.
BTs TSR improvement in the 2012 financial year is mainly due to the increase in the share price during 2012, from a closing price of 184.7p on 31 March 2011.
Over the last five financial years BTs TSR was negative 2.7%, compared with the FTSE100 TSR of positive 10.2% and the FTSEurorst 300 Telco Index TSR of positive 4.5%.
