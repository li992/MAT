a Summary of main schemes The Companys employees participate in the following Group defined benefit pension schemes: Centrica Pension Plan CPP, Centrica Pension Scheme CPS, Centrica Engineers Pension Scheme CEPS and Centrica Unfunded Pension Scheme.
