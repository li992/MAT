Derivative financial instruments and hedge accounting The Group uses derivative financial instruments to manage its exposure to movements in oil and gas prices, interest rates and foreign exchange.
The Group does not use derivatives for speculative purposes.
Derivative financial instruments are stated at fair value The purpose for which a derivative is used is established at inception.
