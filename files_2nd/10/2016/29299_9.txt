Key business risks are formally identified, reviewed and updated by the Group Executive Committee GEC every six months using a risk scoring methodology.
Each risk is categorised based on likelihood and potential impact.
Once agreed with the GEC, the risks are plotted on a risk matrix and submitted to the Audit Committee for approval and subsequently to the Board.
Mitigating controls are identified and opportunities for the enhancement of the Groups control environment are implemented.
