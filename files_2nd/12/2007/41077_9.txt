Staff and customer surveys Each year the Group conducts surveys of both its staff and its customers.
The staff survey was completed by some two-thirds of staff, and showed very positive results on the existence and communication of the Groups strategy and values, on personnel practices, training and development and what it is like to work in the Group.
The customer survey was completed by approximately 15% of customers 551.
Key indicators from this, which are related to the performance of staff, show that 90% of customers are satisfied with the contact they have with their local Workspace representative, and with the Group overall as their landlord 91%.
Furthermore, nearly 95% of respondents agreed that Workspace staff are committed, reliable and honest.
These results show a direct link between our human resource policies and customer satisfaction.
