Management of sustainability The Board has ultimate responsibility for establishing Group policies relating to sustainability and ensuring that appropriate standards are met.
The CSR Committee has been established as a formal committee of the Board with delegated responsibility for various sustainability issues, focusing on compliance and ensuring that appropriate systems and practices are in place Group-wide to ensure the effective management of  risks.
As Chairman of the CSR Committee, Graham Birch has Board level responsibility for sustainability issues to whom the Vice President of Operations and the Vice President of Legal and Corporate Affairs report for sustainability issues.
