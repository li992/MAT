JLT employees take real pride in exercising their personal responsibility to make a positive contribution to the environment and the communities in which we live and work.
JLTs support for the Udaan Foundation, which brings educational opportunity to deprived children in Mumbai, extends to colleagues from around the world.
Dedicated volunteers from our Mumbai offices regularly take time to support both teachers and school children, enriching the learning environment and bringing experiences such as sports and school trips to the Foundation.
At the same time, our colleagues internationally raise significant new funds for the Foundation, and provide support in many other ways.

Our Local Community Insurance Service LCIS team in JLT Australia, for example, delivers low cost access to insurance services for small not-for-profit organisations and clubs who would otherwise face insurmountable obstacles to hosting events and accepting volunteers.
Through our role in delivering employee benefits through our BenPal platform for hundreds of thousands of employees of our clients, we are also able to promote and deliver schemes such as the UK Cycle to Work scheme to a wider target group, giving them access to healthier lifestyle options.
