OVERVIEW OF FINANCIAL RESULTS Executing our strategy The Group has delivered another strong year of strategic progress and excellent earnings growth, in what remains a challenging and uncertain macroeconomic backdrop, both globally and in the Caucasus region.
We are confident however in our ability to continue to deliver high returns and strong performances in both the Banking Business and the Investment Businesses during 2017 and beyond.
