Our purpose is to help our customers run their businesses more effectively, helping them to gain greater insight into their business activities and providing them with lasting benefits by automating their business processes.
We offer high levels of customer service and our customer-centric approach is designed to engender customer loyalty, whilst at the same time setting us apart from our competition.
The quality of our products and service encourages recommendation by our customers, as well as by over 40,000 accountants in practice.
We continue to expand the Group through acquiring businesses either in new territories or to complement our activities in existing territories.
We add value to new acquisitions by bringing operational excellence to their local initiatives and helping them to introduce models to manage their customer service and support-related activities more effectively.
Our devolved organisational structure is based on nurturing the entrepreneurship, innovation and team spirit of our people, allowing us to leverage the power of local expertise.
