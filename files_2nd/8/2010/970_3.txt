Barro Alto The Barro Alto project is located in the state of Gois, Brazil, approximately 170 km from Anglo Americans existing Codemin nickel operation.
The project was approved in December 2006 and will begin production in March 2011.
Average production will be 36ktpa of nickel over the life of the mine, with an average of 41 ktpa over the first five years.
Once at full production, the operation is expected to be in the lower half of the cash cost curve, and will more than double production from Anglo Americans Nickel business.
