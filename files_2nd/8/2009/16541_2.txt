Overview 02 Vedanta Resources plc Annual report 2009 Vedanta at a Glance Vedanta Resources plc Vedanta is a London listed FTSE 100 diversi ed metals and mining major.
The group produces aluminium, copper, zinc, lead, iron ore and commercial energy.
Vedanta has operations in India, Zambia and Australia and a strong organic growth pipeline of projects.
With an empowered talent pool of nearly 30,000 employees globally, Vedanta places strong emphasis on partnering with all its stakeholders based on the core values of entrepreneurship, excellence, trust, inclusiveness and growth.
