BUNZL We are a growing and successful Group with operations across the Americas, Europe and Australasia.
We supply a variety of products that are everyday essentials for our customers to operate their businesses.
By outsourcing the purchasing, consolidation and distribution of a broad range of everyday items, our customers are able to focus on their core businesses, achieve purchasing efficiencies and savings, free up working capital, improve distribution capabilities and simplify their internal administration.
