Highlights of our Year Invensys has made progress on a number of financial priorities setout at the start of the year.
Each of our divisions has continued toprogress and expand their presence in key growth markets.
This year has seen continued investment in new product development that should benefit each of our divisions in futureyears.
We maintained good cash and cost management discipline.
The Group remains debt free.
Total dividends for the year were raised from 3.0 pence per share last year to 4.0 pence per share.
We also continued to support the principles of environmental stewardship and social responsibility.
