BUSINESS OVERVIEW AT-A-GLANCE IMI plc is the ultimate holding company of the Group and has been listed on the London Stock Exchange since 1966.
At 31 December 2011 it had a market value of 2.4bn and shareholders funds of 565m.
15,700 employees provide innovative knowledge-based engineering solutions for market-leading customers around the world.
The Companys headquarters is at Lakeside, Birmingham Business Park, England.
The trading activities of the IMI Group are conducted through subsidiary companies.
