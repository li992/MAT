Introduction John Laing Infrastructure Fund Limited is a Guernsey registered closed-end investment company with a Primary Listing on the London Stock Exchange.
The Company listed in November 2010, raising the full target of 270 million and acquired a Seed Portfolio of 19 assets within 22 days.
In 2011 the Company raised a further 158 million, used to acquire a mixture of new PPP investments and additional shareholdings in existing assets: entering the FTSE 250 in December 2011.
The Company has a 1 Portfolio of 30 low risk operational, availability based, PPP global infrastructure assets primarily sourced from the John Laing Group, with a growing number of investments acquired from 1 third parties in the broader secondary market.
