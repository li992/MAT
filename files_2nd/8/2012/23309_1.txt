The Edinburgh Investment Trust plc is an investment trust whose investment objective is to invest primarily in UK securities with the long-term objective of achieving: 1. an increase of the Net Asset Value per share by more than the growth in the FTSE All-Share Index: and 2. growth in dividends per share by more than the rate of UK inflation.
At 31 March 2012, the Company had net assets of 982.2 million.
