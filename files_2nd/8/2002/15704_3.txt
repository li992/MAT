Tibbett & Britten at a glance Although originally founded in the UK, Tibbett & Britten has successfully established two home markets Europe and North America whilst selectively developing elsewhere.
The Group generated 95% of its 2002 turnover of 1.5bn in its core geographical sectors and now employs 38,500 people across 34 countries.
