04 GKN plc 2002 HIGHLIGHTS 2002: hard won achievement in uncertain times Increase of 9% in profit before tax, goodwill amortisation and exceptional items in demanding economic environment.
Torque Systems Group addresses advanced driveline systems market.
Acquisition of 33.3% of Japanese torque management leader.
Improved performance in Powder Metallurgy.
New Hoeganaes plant in full production.
Steady improvements in the US.
Robust performance by OffHighwaySystems and AutoComponents.
Two US aerospace acquisitions bring new technologies.
GKNs composite technology wins first business on F-35 and A380.
Restructuring of Aerospace Services in response to civil downturn.
Effect of downturn mitigated by strong focus on military programmes.
AgustaWestland teams with Lockheed Martin to take EH101 into the US.
Full year dividend per share of 11.3p is 3% higher than 2001.
Solid balance sheet and strong cash flow.
Average maturity of Group borrowings extended to 11 years.
