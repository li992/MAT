Charter at a glance Charter, headquartered in London, owns through a number of intermediate companies two engineering businesses, one focused on welding, cutting and automation ESAB, and the other on air and gas handling Howden.
Both ESAB and Howden are established world leaders, supplying performance critical components to end-users.
