Annual Report and Accounts 2007 01 Our focus Our businesses Our focus is on maximising the value of where the land meets the sea.
This is the area from which we operate the ports that serve the UKs trading routes, as well as manage and develop our shoreline property assets.
We leverage our assets to drive commercial and economic growth.
