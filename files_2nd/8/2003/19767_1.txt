Highlights New bmibaby base at Cardiff Cardiff International Airport became bmibabys second UK base in November 2002.
Initially flying over 100 times a week, the airline carried some 180,000 passengers in the five months to March 2003.
Covered walkway opened at Luton A 5 million covered walkway and passenger holding areas were completed at London Luton, improving the operational efficiency of the airport and the quality of customers experience.
Ryanair base at Stockholm Skavsta Ryanair established a base at Stockholm Skavsta in April 2003, with an immediate anticipated increase in annual passenger numbers from 0.3 million in 2003 to 1.5 million in 2004.
