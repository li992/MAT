01 Corporate Profile & Financial Highlights Daily Mail and General Trust is one of the longest-established and most successful media companies in the UK.
Its origins date back to the launch in 1896 of the Daily Mail and it now has interests around the world in national and regional newspapers, business publishing, television, radio, exhibitions, business to business information and careers.
Recent investments put DMGT at the forefront of new and exciting media developments.
It is our intention to build on our long-standing commitment to quality and our unrivalled editorial excellence and experience to ensure that we continue to maintain the best media products of tomorrow.
