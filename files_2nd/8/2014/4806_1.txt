Diploma PLC uses alternative performance measures as key financial indicators to assess the underlying performance of theGroup.
These include adjusted operating profit, adjusted profit before tax, adjusted earnings per share, free cash flow, trading capital employed and return on adjusted trading capital employed ROATCE.
The narrative in the Annual Report & Accounts is based on these alternative measures and an explanation is set out in note 2 tothe consolidated financial statements included in the Annual Report & Accounts.
