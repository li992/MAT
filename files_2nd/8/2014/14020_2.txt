2 Annual Report & Accounts 2014 group at a glance Senior is an international, market-leading, engineering solutions provider with 32 operations in 14 countries.
Senior designs, manufactures and markets high-technology components and systems for the principal original equipment producers in the worldwide aerospace, defence, land vehicle and energy markets.
The Group is split into two divisions, Aerospace and Flexonics, servicing five key sectors.
