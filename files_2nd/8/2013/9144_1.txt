Kier Group plc Annual Report & Accounts 2013 A strong platform for future growth Kier Group plc Annual Report & Accounts 2013 Kier Group plc is a leading construction, services and property group specialising in building and civil engineering, support services, commercial property development, structured property financing and private and affordable housing.
The Group employs over 16,000 people worldwide and has an annual revenue of 2.8bn.
By providing our clients with the highest possible quality of service and through sustainable, profitable growth, our vision is to be the most highly respected company in the industry.
