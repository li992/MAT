g Policy on contracts of employment The Companys policy regarding contracts of employment is that all senior employees, including Executive Directors, should be offered rolling contracts of no longer than twelve months.
Where it is commercially appropriate to protect the Company, a longer-term initial contract with any employee, including an Executive Director, may be entered into.
On completion of the initial contract, the Companys standard terms will be applied.
The Remuneration Committee, in considering contracts, has regard to compensation commitments in respect of termination and believes that these are best addressed by restricting the term of the contract.
In the event of a termination, the Remuneration Committee would consider all the relevant factors and seek a just solution.
