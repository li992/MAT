Contingent share awards were made under the LTIPs during the years ended 31 May 2005, 2007 and 2008 which vest on publication of the results for the financial year to 31 May 2008, 2009 and 2010 respectively.
The maximum participation limit of the LTIP scheme is 10% of the issued share capital in a ten year rolling period.
