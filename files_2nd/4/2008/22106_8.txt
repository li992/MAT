Candover 26 Report and accounts 2008 Non-executive directors letters of appointment Each non-executive director has a letter of appointment with the Company.
All current non-executive directors letters of appointment have been updated and reissued during 2008 2009 to bring them in line with best practice.
Non-executive directors appointments are reviewed annually and are subject to the re-election requirements of the Companys Articles of Association.
There are no provisions for directors to receive compensation upon early termination.
