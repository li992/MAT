k Policy on external appointments Executive Directors are allowed to accept external appointments as a non-Executive Director of up to two other companies provided that these are not with competing companies and are not likely to lead to conflicts of interest.
In normal circumstances, the Group Chief Executive may not accept more than one external appointment.
Executive Directors are normally allowed to retain the fees paid from these appointments.
Currently no Executive Director has an external appointment.
