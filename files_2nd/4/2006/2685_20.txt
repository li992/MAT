The vesting of options awarded under the SOP is subject to relative TSR performance conditions.
Full vesting occurs only if the Companys TSR performance ranks at or above the upper quartile of a comparator group comprising FTSE Global Telecoms companies.

A sliding scale operates between median and upper quartile, and nothing vests for TSR performance below the median.
For awards granted after May 2004, performance will be measured over a single three year performance period.
For earlier grants, performance is able to be re-tested until the fifth anniversary of the grant.
