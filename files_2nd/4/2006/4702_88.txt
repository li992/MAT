The current performance condition is based on the increase in Diageo's EPS over a three year period.
If the increase in this EPS measure is at least 15 percentage points greater than the increase in the UK Retail Prices Index RPI over the same period, then all the options can be exercised.
If the increase in this EPS measure is at least 12 percentage points greater than that of the RPI but less than 15 percentage points, half of the options can be exercised.
In respect of the 2003 SESOP grant, the performance condition will be re-tested in 2007.
The facility to re-test was removed in October 2004 and as such this is the last grant eligible for re-testing under the Rules of the Plan.
