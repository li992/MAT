Non-executive Directors shareholdings Non-executive Directors are required to achieve a minimum shareholding of 30% of base fees within three years of appointment.
The shareholding requirement is tested annually on 31 December using the average share price for the final three months of the year based only on shares held outright by the Directors or their connected persons.
All non-executive Directors met the requirement comfortably.
