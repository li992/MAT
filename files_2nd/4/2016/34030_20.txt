Remuneration Policy Our Remuneration Policy was last approved by shareholders at the 2014 AGM and in accordance with the regulations on Directors remuneration, it will remain valid for a period of three years subject to there being no changes required.
We reviewed the Remuneration Policy during FY16, taking into account the current strategy of the Company, and agreed that it remains fit for purpose.

We are also conscious of the Governments new intentions to make changes to executive remuneration.
We will monitor these closely and will seek guidance on the implementation of any changes from our remuneration consultants as well as consult with you as necessary when the details are published.
