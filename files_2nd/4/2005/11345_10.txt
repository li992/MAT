49 Summary of LTIP Maximum award 75% of salary permitted.
Performance conditions against Comparison of TSR with two comparator groups over three years: Total Shareholder Return TSR group 1 70% of award depends on performance against this group.
30% vests at median performance with sliding scale increase to 100% when top of group.
group 2 30% of award depends on performance against this group.
30% vests when TSR equals the index, sliding scale increase to 100% when TSR is at least 6% above the index.
Where the Companys performance is below the median performance of the comparator group, none of that element of the award will vest.
Composition of comparator group 1 listed water companies: AWG plc, Bristol Water plc, East Surrey Holdings plc groups see note below, Kelda Group plc, Pennon Group plc, Severn Trent plc and United Utilities plc.
group 2 FTSE 250 Total Return Index, excluding investment trusts.
Note: The Board has noted the proposed acquisition of East Surrey Holdings plc by Kellen Acquisitions Limited.
If the proposed acquisition proceeds, the Board will take independent advice on the impact on the composition of the comparator group and the calculation of TSR.
In addition, awards will only vest if the Committee is satisfied that the Companys TSR performance is consistent with the underlying business performance of the Company.
In the event of a change of control, the Board would determine the extent to which the performance conditions had been met and the proportion of the performance period that had elapsed in deciding whether or not any vesting of awards would take place.
The performance conditions described above were chosen to align the interests of participants in the LTIP with those of shareholders.
An independent firm of chartered accountants will be engaged to calculate the TSR and to assess the extent to which the performance conditions have been met, so that the process is rigorous and transparent.
