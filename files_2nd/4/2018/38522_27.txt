Directors Service Contracts None of the Directors have a contract of service with the Company.
Each Director has entered into terms of appointment as a Non-Executive Director of the Company.
There has been no other contract or arrangement between the Company and any Director at any time during the year.
Under the Articles of Association, each Director shall retire and be subject to re-appointment at the first AGM following appointment, and at least every three years thereafter.
After nine years service, Directors are subject to annual re-appointment.
Following the Companys inclusion in the FTSE 250 all Directors will be subject to annual re-election at each AGM.
There are no agreements between the Company and its Directors concerning compensation for loss of office.
