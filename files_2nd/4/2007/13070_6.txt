In 2007, Reckitt Benckiser continued to perform very well against its peers, and exceeded the maximum stretch targets designed to achieve maximum bonus payments.
Bonus payments for Executive Directors accordingly increased by an average of 18%.
This increase is due to above industry average performance for the core business operations, combined with a highly successful integration of Boots Healthcare International BHI which delivered synergies ahead of target.
