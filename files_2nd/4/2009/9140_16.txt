FINANCIAL STATEMENTS DIRECTORS REMUNERATION REPORT 50 51 Non-executive directors The remuneration of the non-executive directors is determined by the Board and reflects the anticipated time commitment to fulfil their duties.
Non-executive directors do not receive bonuses, long-term incentive awards, pension provision or compensation on termination of their appointment.
The Company has agreements for services with each of the non-executive directors, all of which are terminable on no more than 12 months notice by either party.
