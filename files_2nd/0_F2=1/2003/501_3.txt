Locations Aggreko is a locally-focused business, that has a global reach through an international network spanning North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Australia.
With more than 100 service centres in 28 countries, we combine local knowledge with global capability.
Our staff have on-theground experience in their own markets, while learning from the work of colleagues all over the world.
Being close to our customers means we can be there in an emergency, able to respond quickly to their needs.
At the same time, as a global business we can use our resources strategically, moving staff and equipment around the world to wherever our customers need them.
