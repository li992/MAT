2003 operating performance Earnings in 2003 at US$17 million were 21 per cent higher than the previous year.
Luzenacs production in 2003 was two per cent higher than 2002 at 1.36 million tonnes.
Sales volumes were maintained from 2002, while improvements in pricing were partially offset by the effect of the sales mix.
