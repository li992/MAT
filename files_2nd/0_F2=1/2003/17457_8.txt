33 Xansa Report and Accounts 2003 The Xansa 1995 Qualifying Employee Share Ownership Trust The Xansa QUEST was established in June 1995 as the FI 1995 Qualifying Employee Share Ownership Trust when it acquired 15,198,000 shares from Dame Stephanie Shirley at 17p per 5p ordinary share, such price being determined by independent valuation.
The Xansa QUEST was established under the provisions of the 1989 Finance Act.
The beneficiaries of the Xansa QUEST are employees of Xansa in the UK with at least 12 months service.
If shares are ultimately transferred or sold to beneficiaries they must be made available to all beneficiaries on similar terms.
The Xansa QUEST is key in helping to maintain a significant level of share ownership by the employees which is an important element in retaining high levels of motivation.
