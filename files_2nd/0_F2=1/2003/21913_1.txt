British Empire Securities and General Trust p. l. c. Company Summary The Company The Company is an investment trust and its shares are listed on the London Stock Exchange.
It is a member of the Association of Investment Trust Companies.
The Groups net asset value at 30 September 2003 was 348 million and the market capitalisation was 327 million.
Objective The investment objective of the Company is to achieve capital growth through a focused portfolio of investments, particularly in companies whose share prices stand at a discount to estimated underlying net asset value.
