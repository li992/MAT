Policy and Objective Scottish Mortgage is one of the largest investment trust companies in the UK.
Its objective is to maximise total return to shareholders at the same time as generating real dividend growth.
It invests internationally, mainly in quoted securities.
Its portfolio is concentrated in a relatively small number of holdings.
These are mainly in strong businesses with good prospects for growth.
During the year the Company bought back 11,179,573 shares for cancellation.
