ABERFORTH PARTNERS INFORMATION Aberforth Partners are Managers and Secretaries to the Company.
Aberforth Partners were established in 1990 to provide institutional and wholesale investors with a high level of resources entirely focused on small UK quoted companies.
Since then funds under management have grown to approximately 1.3 billion as at 31 December 2003.
Aberforth Partners are wholly owned by six partners five founding partners who are investment managers and a sixth partner, Gordon R Young, who is responsible for administration.
The rm has two additional investment managers and the seven work as a team managing the Companys portfolio on a collegiate basis.
The founding partners have been managing the portfolio since the Companys inception in December 1990.
These partners each have a substantial personal investment in the Company.
