McCarthy & Stone plc Directors Report The Directors of McCarthy & Stone plc present their annual report to shareholders, together with the audited accounts for the year ended 31 August 2003.
Principal activity and The Group is engaged principally in developing, designing, constructing and selling retirement review of the business apartments.
Reviews of the Group's business during the year, together with comments on the future development of the Group, are set out on pages 5 to 15 and 22 to 23.
Results and dividends The Group profit before tax for the financial year amounted to 116.0m 75.4m an increase of 54%.
The Group incurred a tax charge of 34.8m in respect of the year 22.7m.
The Directors recommend a final ordinary dividend of 9.8 pence 8.1 pence per share making 13.7 pence for the year 11.4 pence.
The ordinary dividend amounts to 14.2m 11.7m which when combined with the preference dividend of 2.0m 2.0m leaves a profit of 65.0m 39.0m to be added to reserves.
The final ordinary dividend, subject to approval by shareholders, will be paid on 16 December 2003 to those on the register at 14 November 2002.
