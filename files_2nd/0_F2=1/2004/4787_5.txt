21 operational review Protocol Protocol Venture Capital was established in 2001 as Dimension Datas Business Development Fund, with the objective of establishing strategic joint ventures that would enhance relationships with suppliers and clients and foster new entrepreneurial businesses complementary to the Groups existing business.
The strategy is to continue to create and preserve value in existing Protocol portfolio companies and to exit them over time at fair values.
Total investments under management at year end were 11 compared with 17 as at 30 September 2003.
At the year end the Protocol Investments were carried at a book value of US$14.2 million compared to US$16.9 million at 30 September 2003.
