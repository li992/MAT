com Directors report Principal activities The principal activities of the Group are Retailing and Financial Services.
Retailing consists of the Groups retail activities under the Marks & Spencer and Kings Super Markets brand names.
Financial Services consists of the operations of the Groups retail financial services companies, which provide credit and account cards, personal loans, unit trust management, life assurance, personal insurance and pensions.
The Groups captive insurance company is also included in this segment as the major part of its business is generated from the provision of related insurance services.
