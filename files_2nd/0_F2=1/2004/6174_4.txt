Automated paper handling Tilbury Within Project Enterprise the opportunity to use the latest innovations in supply chain technology has been exploited to create the worlds first port based fully integrated automated paper handling and storage facility.
Robotic technology utilising advanced software controls, laser and optical technology enables reels of 1.8 tonnes to be located, identified sorted and moved in seconds.
The combined technologies enable one operator to control the receipt, storage and loading of 250,000 tonnes of newsprint per annum, while giving the flexibility to handle virtually any product.
