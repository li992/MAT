The Capita Group Plc Annual Report and Accounts 2007 04 Our business goal and strategy Our goal is to deliver value to all our stakeholders Our straightforward goal is to continue developing Capita as a long term, sustainable business that can deliver value to all its stakeholders: k Delivering operational excellence and added value for all clients kAchieving a good return for shareholders k Creating a supportive, rewarding and inspiring environment for employees k Developing mutually beneficial relationships with suppliers k Making a positive impact on the communities in which we work.
Our strategy is clear, straightforward and pragmatic To meet the needs of all our stakeholders now and into the future, we have created a clear, straightforward business strategy.

Generating profitable growth both organically and through acquisition 1p Securing long term, recurring revenues from new and existing clients p Acquiring small to medium sized businesses that expand our existing capability and take us into new areas.

Targeting growth markets for BPO and professional support services in the UK and Ireland p Seeking the best opportunities across both the public and private sectors p Focusing on our nine chosen market sectors.
Maintaining performance across our divisions by consistently delivering service excellence and sharing Group resources and scale benefits p Aligning each business within our divisions with the Groups overall objectives and strategy p Investing in service and product development, appropriate sales and marketing activities p Maintaining a simple, pragmatic divisional structure to ensure the best resources are deployed Group-wide.
