Directors report The Directors have pleasure in submitting their annual report and financial statements for the year to 30 September 2007.
Principal activity and business review The principal activity of the Group is the provision of asset management services.
Further information on the Groups business, which is required by section 417 of the Companies Act 2006 can be found in the following sections of the Annual report, which are incorporated into this report by reference: Chairmans statement on pages 2 to 4: and Business review, including detail on principal risks and uncertainties, on pages 9 to 19: and Environmental, social and community matters and employees on page 40.
Financial The results for the year are shown in the Group Income Statement on page 51.
An interim ordinary dividend of 2.6p net per share was paid on 13 June 2007.
The Directors recommend a final ordinary dividend of 2.9p net per share, making a total of 5.5p net per share for the year to 30 September 2007.
The proposed final dividend, if approved, will be paid on 23 January 2008 to shareholders on the register at the close of business on 14 December 2007.
The total distribution of dividends for the year to 30 September 2007 is 39.4 million.

Directors indemnities The Company maintains directors and officers liability insurance which gives appropriate cover for any legal action brought against its directors.
Policy on payment of creditors As required by the Financial Services Authority, all open end fund repurchase settlements are paid within four working days of receipt of the appropriate documentation from investors.
Unless otherwise agreed payments to other creditors are made within thirty days of receipt of an invoice.
At the year end, the Group and Company had an average of 24 days purchases outstanding in respect of trade creditors and an average of four days outstanding in respect of approved unit trust repurchase settlements.
