24 RIGHTMOVE PLC ANNUAL REPORT 2007 Post balance sheet event On 28 January 2008, the Company announced the completion of a Scheme of Arrangement.
Rightmove Group plc became the holding company of Rightmove plc and its subsidiaries.
Under the Scheme of Arrangement, Rightmove Ordinary Shareholders received one Rightmove Group Ordinary Share for each Rightmove Ordinary Share held at the Scheme Record Time.
Rightmove Group plc issued ordinary share capital was admitted to the Ofcial List of the UK Listing Authority and to trading on the main market for listed securities of the London Stock Exchange plc on 28 January 2008 and the listing of Rightmove plc Ordinary Shares was cancelled.
Subsequent to the new listing, Rightmove Group plc Company no: 6426485 changed its name to Rightmove plc.
With effect from 28 January 2008, Rightmove plc Company no: 3997679 changed its name to Rightmove Group Limited.
References to the Company throughout this Annual Report refer to the new parent company Rightmove plc Company no: 6426485 from 28 January 2008 onwards and prior to that, the previous company.
There were no changes to the board of directors as a result of the Scheme of Arrangement and the new listing.
