He was formerly Chairman of the Mental Health Foundation and a non-executive director of Vendme Luxury Group plc between 1993 and 1998.
Sarah Bates 49 I R Non-executive Director Sarah joined the Board as an independent non-executive Director in September 2004 and is a member of the Investment and Risk Committees.
