It is a member of the Association of Investment Companies.
The Groups net asset value at 30 September 2007 was 815million and the market capitalisation was 762 million.
Objective The investment objective of the Company is to achieve capital growth through a focused portfolio of investments, particularly in companies whose share prices stand at a discount to estimated underlying net asset value.
