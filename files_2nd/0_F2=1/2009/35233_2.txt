Our principal objective Our principal objective is to add value to our properties to deliver above average long-term returns to shareholders.
We accomplish this by focusing on four strategic aims: sourcing interesting buildings where we can identify and create value: devising tailor-made planning solutions for each property and working closely with architects and designers to optimise innovative design: focusing on enterprising portfolio management: and maintaining financial stability through flexible funding and timely recycling of capital.
