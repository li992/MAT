Acquisitions and disposals On 1 July 2008 the Group combined its shipbuilding and naval support businesses with those of BAE Systems plc to create a joint venture known as BVT Surface Fleet Limited, in which the Group is a 45 per cent shareholder.
As detailed in note 5 to the consolidated financial statements, the Group intends to exercise its put option over BVT Surface Fleet during summer 2009 and thereby dispose of its interest in the joint venture.
On 1 July 2008 the Group acquired BAE Systems plcs 50 per cent shareholding in Flagship Training Limited giving the Group 100 per cent ownership.

Change of control The Group has a number of borrowing facilities which contain provisions that, following a change of control, give the lender the right to cancel the facility and require immediate repayment of outstanding amounts.
Certain of the Groups trading contracts contain provisions entitling the counterparties to exercise termination, alteration or other similar rights in the event of a change of control of the Company.
In addition, the Companys share plans contain provisions, as a result of which options and awards may vest and become exercisable on change of control of the Company.
Supplier payment policy The Groups policy is to make payment to suppliers either as agreed in any contract with those suppliers or, where no agreement has been made, to make payment within 45 days of receipt of invoice.
We continue to monitor supplier payment terms at both Group and divisional level.
Where we find discrepancies we do our best to ensure they are rectified promptly.
