14 Smiths Group plc Annual report 2009 Improving cash generation through better working capital management is another primary Generating focus.
During the year, we successfully lowered inventories and reduced debtors to improve operating cash conversion from 72% to 104% cash and free cash-flow from 91m to 256m.
At the same time, we are optimising the Groups capital structure and securing its long-term financing.
In the year we raised $675m of longterm debt capital through a private placement and US bond offer.
This extended the maturity of the Groups debt profile and reduced our dependency on the banking market.
