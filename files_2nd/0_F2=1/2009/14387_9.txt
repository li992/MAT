13 Smiths Group plc Annual report 2009 An Indufil filtration system which has expanded the John Crane portfolio A key objective for the Group is to optimise the allocation of capital between the different Reshaping businesses.
We are reshaping the portfolio to drive higher returns and to expand our addressable markets.
In two years we have made the portfolio 10 acquisitions that have added complementary technologies, leveraged our infrastructure and extended our geographic reach.
At the same time, we have rationalised the portfolio through divestments and by reviewing profitability of stock keeping units in businesses such as Smiths Medical.
This reduces complexity and removes low-margin, low-volume parts of the business to boost margins and returns in the medium term.
