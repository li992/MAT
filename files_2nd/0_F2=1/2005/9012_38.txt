Regulatory Financial Review Kingston Kingston Wholesale Market : Asymmetric Broadband Origination in the Hull Area The asymmetric broadband origination market includes all broadband internet access products available to both consumers and businesses in the Hull regulatory area.
Demand for broadband services increased customer numbers in 2004-05 to 17,338 2003-04: 6,331 however revenues did not achieve the same levels of growth.
Revenue derived at a regulatory return on M. C. E. of 13% fell to 2.2m 2003-04: 2.4m, as increases in M. C. E. were offset by improved operating cost performance.
Improved performance on operating costs resulted from operating costs increasing at a slower rate than customer numbers and decreased holding losses of assets.
This market area is expected to continue to show significant growth in future operating periods.
