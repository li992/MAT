SHARES AND SHAREHOLDERS As at the year end, there were 46,754,919 ordinary shares and 40,000,000 C shares in issue.
The share capital and rights attaching to the shares are set out in Note 13 to the financial statements.
The conversion of the C shares on 21 March 2016 resulted in the issue of 39,551,884 ordinary shares on 22 March 2016, with a total of 86,306,803 ordinary shares of 0.01 each in issue as of the date of this report.
Share Issues At the Annual General Meeting held on 15 June 2015, the Directors were granted the authority to allot ordinary shares up to an aggregate nominal value of 20,000.
1,999,999 ordinary shares, with a nominal value of 19,999.99, were issued under this authority at a price of 10.75 per share by way of a Tap Issue of shares which were admitted to trading on the London Stock Exchange on 22 June 2015.
The authority will be renewed at the Companys Annual General Meeting to be held on 9 June 2016.
During the year, the Company undertook two C share issues, details as follows: n Under authority to allot C shares up to a nominal value amount of 20,000,000, representing 200,000,000 C shares, granted to the Directors at a General Meeting on 25 April 2014, 25,000,000 C shares of 0.10 each were issued under a Placing and Intermediaries Offer at an issue price of 10.00, raising an aggregate of 250 million of gross proceeds for the Company.
The C shares commenced trading on the London Stock Exchange on 29 January 2015. n On 22 July 2015, the C shares were converted into ordinary shares at the ratio of 0.9902 ordinary shares for every C share, resulting in the issue of 24,754,920 new ordinary shares.
n Under authority to allot C shares up to a nominal value amount of 20,000,000, representing 200,000,000 C shares granted to the Directors at a General Meeting on 15 June 2015, 40,000,000 C shares of 0.10 each were issued under a Placing and Intermediaries Offer at an issue price of 10.00, raising an aggregate of 400 million of gross proceeds for the Company.
The C shares commenced trading on the London Stock Exchange on 28 July 2015.
There are no restrictions concerning the transfer of securities in the Company or on voting rights: no special rights with regard to control attached to securities: no agreements between holders of securities regarding their transfer known to the Company: and no agreements which the Company is party to that might affect its control following a successful takeover bid.
Purchase of Own Shares At the Annual General Meeting held on 15 June 2015, the Directors were granted the authority to buy back up to 2,998,000 ordinary shares.
During the year the Company has not bought back any shares but intends to seek approval from the shareholders, by special resolution, for authority to purchase a maximum number of 12,937,389 ordinary shares of 0.01 each in the capital of the Company.
The authority will expire on the date of the next Annual General Meeting in 2017.
The board may use its discretion during periods of market dislocation to opportunistically buy-back shares for its treasury account where it believes such a purchase would be accretive over and above the long term attractions of investing in its loan and equity portfolios and it is in the best long term interest of all shareholders to do so.
