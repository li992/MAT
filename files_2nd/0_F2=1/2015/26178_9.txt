Peterborough Schools Peterborough United Kingdom This project involved the construction of the Voyager School in Walton, and extensions to the Jack Hunt School in Netherton and the Ken Stimpson School in Werrinton.
Construction of the schools started in 2006 such that the buildings were available for use for the September 2007 school term.
The schools provide quality teaching space for up to 3,935 pupils.
M40 Motorway Midlands United Kingdom This project involves the widening between junctions 1A to 3 and maintenance of 123km of motorway between junctions 1A to 15.
