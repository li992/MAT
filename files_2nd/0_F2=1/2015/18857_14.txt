Overview Group Key Performance Indicators Johnson Matthey uses a range of key performance indicators KPIs to monitor the groups performance over time in line with its strategy.
These include key measures of our financial performance as well as indicators to monitor ongoing investment in facilities and in R&D.
In addition, we also use KPIs to track the carbon footprint of our operations and to measure and drive continuous improvement in the safety, wellbeing and development of our people.
