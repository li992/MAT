Governance Content Section at start: Page Title at start: Annual Report & Accounts 2016 Remuneration report How shareholders views are taken into account The Committee considers shareholder feedback received regarding the Remuneration report annually and guidance from shareholder representative bodies more generally.
These views are key inputs when shaping remuneration policy.
The Committee consults with shareholders when considering changes to remuneration arrangements.
