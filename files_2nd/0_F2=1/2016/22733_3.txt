In the late 1980s the Board embarked on a strategy to broaden the base of the Groups OUR GROWTH AND activities, developing beyond primary pork processing into added-value pork products and then into other related food categories.
Today Cranswick is a leading DEVELOPMENT and innovative British supplier of premium, fresh and added value food products with annual revenues in excess of 1 billion, and operates from fifteen well-invested, highly efficient production facilities in the UK, employing over 8,000 people.
