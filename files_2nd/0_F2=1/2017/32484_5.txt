Risk management and corporate governance 03 Credit ratings In terms of our dual listed companies structure, creditors are ring-fenced to either Investec Limited or Investec plc as there are no crossguarantees between the companies.
Capital and liquidity are prohibited from flowing between the two entities and thus capital and liquidity are not fungible.
As a result, the rating agencies have assigned separate ratings to the significant banking entities within the group, namely Investec Bank plc and Investec Bank Limited.
Certain rating agencies have also assigned ratings to the holding companies, namely, Investec plc and Investec Limited.
