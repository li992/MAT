The business builds and operates communication towers in rural and suburban areas, together with bre-based networks to improve mobile coverage in large buildings and on city streets.
WIG is independent of any network operator and invests in infrastructure that can be accessed by all networks.
3iN acquired a 36% economic interest in WIG, investing 75 million.
WIG diversies 3iNs portfolio and provides exposure to a growing communications infrastructure business.
With its scalable platform and track record of building new infrastructure and making accretive acquisitions, WIG is well placed to target further growth inthe UK and across Europe.
