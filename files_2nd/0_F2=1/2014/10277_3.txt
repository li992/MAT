04 Overview Europe Overlooking the River Thames and close to the ever-fashionable Kings Road, the 158-suite The Chelsea Harbour Hotel became the Groups sixth owned or leased property in London in March 2014.
In October 2014, the Group also acquired its first hotel in Italy, Grand Hotel Palace Rome.
This luxury 87-room property is on the famous Via Veneto.
Both acquisitions are luxurious, recently refurbished five-star hotels, situated in two of the worlds top travel destinations.
They offer high standards of service and comfort to our guests, whilst fitting efficiently within the Groups existing regional management infrastructure.
