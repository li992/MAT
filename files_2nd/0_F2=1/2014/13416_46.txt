Annual incentive For 2015 the Committee approved a maximum annual incentive opportunity for executive directors of 180% of base salary: this is unchanged from the opportunity in 2013 and 2014.
In addition, 25% of any annual incentive earned will be received as a deferred award over Rexam shares, with no further performance conditions save that such shares must be held for a period of not less than three years and the 2015 awards will be subject to the new malus and clawback policy.
