Loulo-Gounkoto gold mine complex The Loulo-Gounkoto complex comprises two adjoining mining permits, Loulo and Gounkoto, and is situated in western Mali, bordering Senegal and adjacent to the Falm River.
Socit des Mines fide Loulo SA Loulo owns the Loulo gold mine and Socit des Mines fide Gounkoto SA Gounkoto owns the Gounkoto gold mine.
Both Loulo and Gounkoto are owned by Randgold 80% and the State of Mali 20%.
