Operations and exploration Kibali gold mine The Kibali gold mine is located in the north east of the DRC, approximately 560km north east of the capital of the Orientale province, Kisangani, 180km west of the Ugandan border town of Arua and 1 800km from the Kenyan port of Mombasa.
The Kibali gold mine is owned by Kibali Goldmines SA Kibali which is a joint venture company between Randgold 45%, AngloGold Ashanti Limited 45% and Socit Minire fide Kilo-Moto SARL SOKIMO 10%.
The mine was developed and is operated by Randgold.
