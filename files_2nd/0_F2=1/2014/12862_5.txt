Based in the UK, it has an established US footprint and growing positions in targeted international markets.
As a people-based business, our service offerings account for the majority of sales.
In addition our products division provides technology-based solutions on a global basis.
Divisions EMEA Services Global Products EMEA Services combines world-leading expertise with unique facilities to provide technical assurance, test and evaluation, and training services.
The division is also a market leader in research and advice in specialist areas such as C4ISR, acquisition services and cyber security.
