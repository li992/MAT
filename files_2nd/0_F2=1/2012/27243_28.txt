DIRECTORS REPORT AND RESPONSIBILITIES STATEMENT CONTINUED Corporate Governance Statement continued d Internal controls Continued appointment of the Investment Manager The Board reviews investment performance at each Board meeting and a formal review of all service providers is conducted annually by the Management Engagement Committee.
As a result of the 2012 annual review it is the opinion of the Directors that the continued appointment of the current Investment Manager on the terms agreed is in the interest of the Companys shareholders as a whole.
The Investment Manager has extensive investment management resources and wide experience in managing investment companies.
