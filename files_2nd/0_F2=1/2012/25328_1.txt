International Public Partnerships Limited Company Overview International Public Partnerships Limited LSE: INPP, WKH &RPSDQ, 133 is a FTSE 250 company that invests in public and social infrastructure projects.
The portfolio consists of 122 individual investment projects in the United Kingdom, Australia, Canada, Germany, France, Belgium, Italy and Ireland.
Examples include schools, hospitals, courthouses, police stations, transport infrastructure, rolling stock, and energy transmission projects.
The Company seeks to provide its shareholders with both a long-term yield and the opportunity for capital growth through investment across both construction and operational phases of infrastructure projects.
The Company aims to provide a medium term return equal to or greater than 8% based on the initial public offer issue price in November 2006 of 100 pence per share.
The Company listed on the London Stock Exchange in November 2006 with a market capitalisation of approximately 300 million.
Since this time the Company has raised over 440 million of additional capital through a C-share capital listing subsequently converted, by way of placements, open offers and offers for subscription and through tap issuance.
