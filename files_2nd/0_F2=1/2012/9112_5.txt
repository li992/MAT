Kentz has retained its strong ability to build physical assets for clients.
Since its beginnings in Ireland providing electrical and instrumentation services during a period of industrial development to the expansion of the Group to international markets in 1977 and its further globalisation to the worldwide presence that it currently has, Kentz has retained its strong ability to build physical assets for clients.
From 1919, Kentz has been a leading provider of construction solutions to clients through the provision of electrical and instrumentation services, with the addition of mechanical, structural and piping services in recent decades.
Advantage The capabilities that Kentz possess to deliver such crucial services for clients on a global level distinguishes it from many of its peers.
The risk in many projects lies with the physical construction of the asset and Kentz has its own construction workforce that has developed the skills and experience to successfully build these assets.
The provision of services to energy and resource companies leads Kentz to remote and challenging locations of the world.
Kentzs success in providing high quality construction services in such difficult locations has allowed it to develop an extensive track record and being one of the few global construction contractors with the capability to deliver high quality end products for its clients.

The leadership team of Kentz is spread throughout the globe, near projects and close toclients.
Unlike many international companies, Kentz does not operate from a central headquarters.
The benets of this operating model canbe seen in the excellent and long-standing relationships that Kentz has developed and maintained with key clients over a long period of time.
The very first client that Kentz worked for when it began its international operations in 1977 continues to be a key client to this day.
The importance of fostering such strong relationships is evident in thelevel of repeat business that Kentz undertakes for core clients and the expansion of its operations to new locations of the world with such clients.
Advantage On a practical level, the main advantages of having a decentralised model are: t Having our senior executives close toprojects and clients assist in the business winning and retaining process as constant dialogue is being maintained and knowledge being shared allowing Kentz to focus on any upcoming client developments.
t Operating on large complex projects in remote and challenging locations means that difficulties and delays have to be overcome from time to time.
