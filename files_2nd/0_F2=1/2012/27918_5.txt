The Paragon Group of Companies PLC Directors report The directors submit their Report and the Accounts for the year ended 30 September 2012 which were approved by the Board on 20 November 2012.
Principal activities The Company registered number 2336032 is a holding company co-ordinating the activities of its subsidiary companies.
