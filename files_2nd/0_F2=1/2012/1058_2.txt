Governance 74 Management team The activities of the mining division are managed by the Antofagasta Minerals corporate centre, under the leadership of Diego Hernndez, the Antofagasta Minerals CEO.
The management team at the Antofagasta Minerals corporate centre comprises eight vice-presidents with core areas of responsibility as set out below.
