A values-led business Serco could not succeed without the skill and dedication of our people around the world.
They embody our culture and our values, which underpin the way we run the Group.
Our values, which are encapsulated in our Governing Principles, inform every decision we make.
They ensure that we deliver the excellent service on which our success depends.
The Governing Principles are integral to the Serco Management System SMS, which is our mandatory management framework within which all parts of Serco must operate.
Each policy area in the SMS reects one or more of our Governing Principles, which means that working within the SMS ensures we are living our values.
