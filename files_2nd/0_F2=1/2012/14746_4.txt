SSE Annual Report 2012 02 The energy sector Global context The production of electricity and gas for customers in the UK and Ireland is a market-based activity in which producers, retailers, large users and other energy traders buy and sell electricity and gas like any other commodity.
As the decline of oil and gas production from the UK Continental Shelf continues, there is a continuing integration of UK energy prices in the wider global market, which means macro economic and geopolitical factors are important.
During 2011 12, the energy market was affected by world events including the Eurozone instability, the Arab Spring and the ongoing effects of the tsunami-hit Fukushima power plant disaster.
