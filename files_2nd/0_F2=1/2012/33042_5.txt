4 Overview Our strategy Our aim Our aim is to drive shareholder value by making BT a better business with a better future.
There are three foundations for making BT a better business.
They build on one another: the better we serve our customers, the less time and money we have to spend on putting things right.
By transforming our costs we create opportunities for investing in our future.
By investing in our six strategic priorities we aim to grow the value of the business.
