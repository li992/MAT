6 Directors report: Business review The Directors present their Annual Report to the shareholders together with the audited financial statements for the financial year ended 28 April 2012.
This report, together with the Chairmans Statement, describes the results and activity for the period, future plans, trends and factors affecting the development, position and performance of the business.
Principal activities Carpetright plc is Europes leading specialist floor covering retailer, selling a wide range of carpets, rugs, vinyls and laminates together with associated accessories.
We have extended our product offering to include beds in around half of our stores.
The Group trades from 632 stores organised and managed in two geographical segments, the UK and the Rest of Europe comprising the Netherlands, Belgium and the Republic of Ireland.
Business objective and strategies The primary financial objective of the Group is to deliver long term sustainable growth in earnings per share and cash flow.
