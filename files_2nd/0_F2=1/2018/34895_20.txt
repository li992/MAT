At the 2019 AGM, the Directors will again seek authority to purchase the Companys own shares.
Employee share scheme The Company has established an Employee Share Trust EST rights for the purpose of facilitating the operation of the Companys share plans.
The EST waives any voting rights and dividends that may be declared in respect of such shares which have not been allocated for the settlement of awards made under the Companys share plans.
Employees may direct the EST as to how to exercise voting rights over shares in which they have a beneficial interest.
