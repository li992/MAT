Directors report The directors have pleasure in presenting their report and the audited financial statements of the group for the year ended 31 December 2008.
Principal activity The company is the ultimate holding company for the Beazley group, a global specialist risk insurance and reinsurance business operating through its managed syndicates 2623, 3623, 3622 and 623 at Lloyds in the UK and BICI, a US-admitted carrier in the US.
