Galiform plc came into existence in October 2006, when MFI Furniture Group plc sold its retail business and subsequently changed its name.
The change of name signifies a new beginning for a group that has, at its heart, two businesses with a unique culture Howden Joinery and Howden Kitchens.
In this report, we want to share with you the reasons why we believe this is a business with a strong future.
