GROUP PROFILE Ceramics Known in its markets as Vesuvius, the Ceramics division is the world leader in the supply of advanced flow control refractory products and systems to the global steel industry and a leading supplier of specialist ceramic products to the glass, solar and foundry industries.
It is also a regional leader in the US, UK and Australia in the supply and installation of monolithic refractory linings.
Electronics The Electronics division is a leading supplier of advanced surface treatment and plating chemicals and assembly materials to the automotive, industrial, construction and electronics markets.
It comprises two sectors: Assembly Materials 58% of revenue and Chemistry 42% of revenue.
Precious Metals The Precious Metals division is a leading supplier of fabricated precious metals primarily gold, silver and platinum to the jewellery industry in the US, UK, France and Spain.
Products include alloy materials, semi-finished jewellery components and finished jewellery.
