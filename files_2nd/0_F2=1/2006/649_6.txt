Employees It is the Groups policy to communicate with and involve employees on matters affecting their interests at work, and inform them of the performance of the business.
In addition to an annual employee survey and a regular employee forum in each Business Stream, this is achieved by publishing a range of internal magazines and newsletters across the Group and holding regular team briengs and toolbox talks.
It is also Group policy to treat all employees and potential employees equally and to give full consideration to suitable applications for employment from disabled persons, where they have the necessary abilities and skills for the position, and wherever possible to re-train employees who become disabled, so that they can continue their employment in another position.
During Autumn 2006, the Alfred McAlpine plc Buy As You Earn Share Scheme, an all employee HMRC approved share incentive plan, which was approved by shareholders in 2000, was brought into operation.
745 employees now participate in the Scheme.
The principal aim of the Scheme is to encourage eligible employees to become shareholders in the Company to align employees interests with those of shareholders.
Further details are set out on page 32 of the Remuneration Report.
The Company also operates an all employee savings-related share option scheme.
At 31 December 2006 there were 413 2005: 1,117 employees participating in this scheme, details of which are set out in note 22 to the Accounts on page 63.
