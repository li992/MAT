Southern Cross Healthcare Group Southern Cross Healthcare is the largest provider of care homes for the elderly in the UK, and a major provider of specialist services for people with physical and or learning disabilities, operating 580 homes with 28,917 beds at 1 October 2006.
Following the listing of the Companys shares on the London Stock Exchange in July 2006, the Company entered the FTSE 250 index in September 2006.
