ALLIED GOLD MINING PLC Sulphide Study and exploration Much of the exploration at Simberi in 2011 focused on metallurgical and exploration core drilling for the Simberi sulphide Bankable Feasibility Study BFS due for completion in 2012.
Simberi already has in excess of one million ounces of sulphides in reserves.
The BFS will review the capital and operating costs of a sulphide roaster processing 2.5 Mtpa of ore producing 130,000- 150,000ozpa over a 710 year period.
Subject to the Board's approval to proceed, the Sulphide Project Development would occur on or around 2015.
