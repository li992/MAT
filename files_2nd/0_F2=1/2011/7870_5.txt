BUSINESS OVERVIEW OUR PERFORMANCE BUSINESS PERFORMANCE IS MEASURED AGAINST FIVE ELEMENTS OF OUR STRATEGY, THROUGH GROUPWIDE TARGETS AND IMPROVEMENT MEASURES.
Each business unit of IMI participates in an annual round of planning meetings with the Executive Committee of the Board, during which performance and future plans for that business are reviewed and updated.
These business plans are all aligned with the Group business strategy and include specic local, regional and sector targets and Key Performance Indicators KPIs which measure business performance.
In addition, individual business reviews take place on a regular basis enabling the Board to review performance against tactical and strategic milestones and most importantly allowing informed decisions to be taken at each level of the organisation.
