Dignity plc Annual Report & Accounts 2011 31 | Going concern The Directors receive and review regularly management accounts, cash balances, forecasts and the annual budget together with covenant reporting.
After careful consideration, and mindful of the current market conditions, the Directors confirm they are satisfied that the Group has adequate resources to continue operating for the foreseeable future.
For this reason, they continue to adopt the going concern basis for preparing the financial statements.
The Directors formally considered this matter at the Board meeting held on 1 March 2012.
