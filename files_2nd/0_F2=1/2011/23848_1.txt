The Investment Objective The investment objective of the Company is to achieve long term capital growth from an actively managed portfolio made up primarily of securities issued by companies listed in China or Hong Kong and Chinese companies listed elsewhere.
It may also invest in listed companies with significant interests in China John Owen, Chairman and Hong Kong.
