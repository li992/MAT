BlueCrest AllBlue Fund Limited the Company ABOUT THE COMPANY continued The Companys Articles incorporate a discount management provision which applies to each class of Share individually that will require a continuation vote to be proposed in respect of the particular class of Shares at a class meeting of the relevant shareholders by way of ordinary resolution if, over the previous 12 month rolling period commencing from 1 January 2008, the relevant class of Shares has traded, on average calculated by averaging the closing mid-market share price on the dates which are 5 Business Days after the date on which each estimated NAV announcement is made for each NAV Calculation Date over the period at a discount in excess of 5 per cent.
to the average Net Asset Value per Share of that class calculated by averaging the NAV per Share of that class as at the NAV Calculation Date at the end of each month during the period.
In the event that a vote to continue is proposed and passed for any class of Ordinary Shares, no further continuation vote will be capable of being proposed for that class for a period of 12 months from the date on which the requirement for a continuation vote was triggered.
