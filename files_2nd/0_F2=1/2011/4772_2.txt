Dignity plcAnnual Report & Accounts 2011 About Dignity Dignity owns 600 funeral locations and operates 35 crematoria in the United Kingdom.
The Group continues to have a strong market presence in pre-arranged funeral plans, where people plan and pay for their funeral in advance.
We are a FTSE 250 company listed on the London Stock Exchange, with over 2,450 employees serving families and local communities across the United Kingdom for generations.
Helping people at one of the most difficult times in their lives remains at the very heart of everything we do.
