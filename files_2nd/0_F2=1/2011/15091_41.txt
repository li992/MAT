Principal activities SuperGroup Plc is a UK based designer of branded premium quality clothing and accessories selling through multiple routes to market including Retail, Wholesale and online.
At the year-end, the Group had 60 standalone retail stores, 75 concessions and a growing number of Wholesale relationships.
Superdry is sold in 85 countries worldwide via its websites and in 40 overseas countries through a well established network of distributors, licensees, agents and franchisees.
