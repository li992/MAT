Scottish and Southern Energy 01 Annual Report 2010 Introduction to SSE What SSE does Directors report Financial statements Shareholder information SSEs core purpose is to provide the energy people need in a reliable and sustainable way.
Its strategy is to deliver sustained real growth in the dividend payable to shareholders through the efficient operation of, and investment in, a balanced range of market-based and  businesses in energy production, storage, distribution, supply and related services, mainly in the UK and Ireland.
This Annual Report sets out what SSE achieved in 2009 10 and its plans for 2010 11 and beyond.
