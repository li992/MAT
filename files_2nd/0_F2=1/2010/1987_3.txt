Berendsen plc Formerly The Davis Service Group Plc Report and Accounts 2010 08 Strategic objectives and key performance indicators Delivering our strategy.
The strategic review has not fundamentally changed our strategic objectives we continue to have four financial objectives and three for corporate responsibility.
However, in order to deliver on the medium-term objectives which were highlighted as part of our strategic review, we have refocused these objectives.
We have also set growth targets and reassessed the key performance indicators which we use to measure progress against our objectives.
The strategy that will help us to achieve these targets is currently being implemented.
Subsequent Annual Reports will provide a true indication of our progress.
