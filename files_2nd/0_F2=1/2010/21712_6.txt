Report on AllBlue Limited AllBlue by BlueCrest Capital Management LLP for the year ended 31 December 2010 AllBlue Limited Class A, USD made 8.06% in the period under review, calendar year 2010.
The performance was strong throughout the year with AllBlue posting positive returns in all quarters and only experiencing two negative months over the year.
All bar one of the underlying funds posted a positive contribution in 2010, with the exception being BlueCube Equity Market Neutral which was slightly down on the year.
AllBlues returns for the calendar year 2010 were driven by BlueCrest Capital International, which generated approximately 50% of the funds return.
Other strong contributions came from BlueTrend, which generated approximately 25% of the funds return and BlueCrest Emerging Markets which generated approximately 15% of the funds return.
Over the year the largest allocation has remained to BlueCrest Capital International, which has seen its weighting range between 30% and 39%, finishing the year at 30.4%.
The other discretionary strategies have seen their allocations increase over the year, BlueCrest Emerging Markets ranged between 13.7% and 18.8%, finishing at 18.5%: BlueCrest Mercantile ranged between 6.5% and 13.6%, finishing at 12.7%: and BlueCrest Multi Strategy Credit ranged between 14.4% and 18.5% finishing at 18.3%.
Within the systematic strategies, BlueTrend ranged between 11.9% and 14.3%, finishing at 14.0%: BlueMatrix ranged between 3.6% and 5.4%, finishing at 3.8%: and BlueCube ranged between 1.8% and 6%, finishing at 1.8%.
AllBlue continued to show low volatility of returns, with the fund delivering a volatility of 3.47% based on weekly estimate data, or 3.24% based on monthly data.
The highest quarterly volatility figure occurred in Q1 4.14% and the lowest occurred in Q3 1.74%.
