12 Premier Farnell Annual Report and Accounts 2010 Strategic Overview: Our Business Our Business Premier Farnell plc is a leading high service, multi-channel distributor.
We purchase electronic components and maintenance and repair products in large volumes from over 3,500 of the worlds leading manufacturers and suppliers.
These large volumes are then distributed in small quantities to our customers which include Electronic Design Engineers, Maintenance, Repair and Operations engineers and purchasing professionals.
By delivering our products the same day or the next day, offering a rich online customer experience, distributing supporting product information and providing software and services that are desirable to our customers, the value that they attribute to our high service proposition is significant.
Our profitability is founded on maintaining our gross margin, which is achieved through delivering our high service offering to customers and managing a cost base that ensures profits are linked closely to sales growth.
Our simple business model allows a high conversion of profits to cash, which can be invested back into our business and distributed to shareholders.
