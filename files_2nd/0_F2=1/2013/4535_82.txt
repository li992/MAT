His agreement is terminable by either party giving not less than 12 months written notice.
If the Company terminates the employment without due notice, other than in circumstances such as gross misconduct or other immediate justifiable cause, the Company is required to make a payment equal to the aggregate of the executive directors basic salary and the value of any contractual benefits for the notice period including any accrued but untaken holiday.
Simon Herrick entered into a service agreement with the Company on 19 October 2011.
If the Company terminates the employment without due notice, other than in circumstances such as gross misconduct or other immediate justifiable cause, the Company is required to make a payment equal to the executive directors basic salary for the notice period including any accrued but untaken holiday.
The Committee generally seeks to apply practical mitigation in respect of termination payments where appropriate.
The service agreements are available to shareholders to view on request from the Company Secretary.
