2 National Grid Gas plc NTS Regulatory Accounting Statements 2012 13 Strategic Review Our business National Grid Gas plc National Grid Gas is a subsidiary of National Grid plc National Grid, based in the UK, where we own and operate regulated gas transmission and distribution networks, and provide gas metering services.
We play a vital role in connecting millions of people safely, reliably and efficiently to the energy they use.
