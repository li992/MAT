18 SuperGroup Plc Annual Report and Financial Statements 2013 Financial Review Introduction The Group has made changes to the way it reports its divisional performances, details of which are included in note 4, segment information.
The changes made provide greater transparency and allow for improved management of divisional performance and replace the previous methodology of reporting based upon the historical management of the corporate entities that comprise the Group.
The principal changes are: inclusion of SuperGroup Europe retail stores in the Retail division: including all trade sales in Wholesale: and non-division specific central overheads have been moved to central costs.
