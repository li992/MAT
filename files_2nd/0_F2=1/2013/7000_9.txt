18 Strategic Pillars Our mission is to Help and Inspire Customers with their Life on the Move.
The existing pillars will evolve into: Supporting Drivers of Every Car: Inspiring Cyclists of Every Age: and Equipping Families for their Leisure Time.
Supporting drivers of every car Through our core categories of Car Maintenance, Car Enhancement and Car Servicing we provide the services and expertise to take the hassle out of motoring.
Inspiring cyclists of every age We deliver a compelling range of own-brand cycles and complement these with a knowledgeable, skilful and competitive cycle-service offering.
Equipping families for their leisure time Through a wide range of Leisure products and accessories, from tents to camping and caravanning accessories, we help customers make the most of their leisure time.
