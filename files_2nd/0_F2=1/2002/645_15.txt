Profits and dividends The Group profit for the year attributable to shareholders amounted to 14.4m 2001: 39.2m restated after tax and goodwill.
The Directors recommend the payment of a final dividend of 5.9p per ordinary share which, together with the interim dividend of 4.1p already paid, makes a total of 10.0p per ordinary share for the financial year.
If approved at the Annual General Meeting AGM to be held on 22nd May, 2003, the final dividend will be paid on 30th May, 2003 to those shareholders on the register at close of business on 9th May, 2003.
After provision for these ordinary dividends and dividends of 0.4m paid to preference shareholders, the retained profit of 3.7m 2001: 28.1m restated has been transferred to reserves.

Employees It is the Groups policy to communicate with and involve employees on matters affecting their interests at work, and inform them of the performance of the business.
It is also Group policy to treat all employees and potential employees equally and to give full consideration to suitable applications for employment from disabled persons, where they have the necessary abilities and skills for the position, and wherever possible to re-train employees who become disabled, so that they can continue their employment in another position.
The Company operates an all-employee savings-related share option scheme, one of the aims of which is to encourage eligible employees to purchase shares in the Company.
At 31st December, 2002 there were 315 2001: 523 employees participating in this scheme, full details of which are set out in note 21 on page 65.
