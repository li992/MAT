In life we must all take responsibility for our actions.
Its the same in business today.
Quality RMC is mindful of the impact its operations have on employees, customers, local communities, the environment and society at large and does everything within its Delivery power to minimise adverse effects, maximise benefits and remediate environmental impacts.
Responsibility We have a long history of corporate responsibility, we strive to operate to the highest standards around the world, and we are continually exploring methods for improving our perfomance for the future.
For the fourth year running, RMC has used the Business in the Environment BiE Index to measure its commitment to environmental management across 35 business units, in all countries of operation.
The average score has risen 60% from 25% in 1999 to 60% in 2002.
