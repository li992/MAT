Michael Gardner 1 July 2000 Appointed a member of the Executive Management Team in 2000.

Michael H C Herlihy 1 January 1996 Appointed a member of the Executive Management Team in 1996.

Rolf Deusinger 1 July 2002 Appointed a member of the Executive Management Team in 2002.
He is Executive Vice President Human Resources.
He joined ICI in October 1999 as Senior Vice President Human Resources for ICI Paints.
Previously he was Head of International Human Resources for Messer Griesheim GmbH based in Germany.
He has over 18 years international experience in the field of Human Resources, predominantly in consumer businesses, including PepsiCo covering the Americas, Europe and Central Asia.
