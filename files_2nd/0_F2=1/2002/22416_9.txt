Portfolio Managers Report continued In general, companies with strong and secure cash flow are favoured in the portfolio and for this reason the portfolio is overweight in the Beverages, Personal Care and Household Products, and Tobacco sectors.
The Oil sector also has defensive characteristics and large holdings are maintained in BP and Shell.
The Pharmaceutical sector has retreated to a low rating by historical standards but the launch of successful new medicines needs to take place for GlaxoSmithKline and AstraZeneca to reverse their recent disappointing share price performances.
The bear market of the last two years has removed from the UK and other stock markets many of the excesses that were a result of the late 1990s speculative bubble.
As investor confidence in public companies returns, the market has a sound valuation platform to make upward progress.
