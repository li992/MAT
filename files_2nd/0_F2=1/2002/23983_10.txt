Board of Directors David Ritchie Chairman Born 1944.
Joined the Board in 1983 and appointed Chairman in December 1998.
He is currently a director of a number of investment trusts and other companies and was formerly a director of Scottish Widows Fund and Life Assurance Society and executive chairman of Scottish Widows Investment Management Limited.
He is chairman of 3i European Technology Trust plc and a director of several investment trusts including The Fleming Japanese Smaller Companies Investment Trust plc.
He was formerly chairman of Fleming Investment Trust Management Limited and a Director of Robert Fleming Holdings.
He is a merchant banker and former chairman of Morgan Grenfell & Co. Limited.
He is a director of a number of companies and for some years was a director of Tokai Bank Europe Ltd, the London-based investment banking subsidiary of the Japanese commercial bank.
Former chairman of Malmgren, Golt, Kingston & Co. Ltd. a leading trade policy consultancy with extensive knowledge of Japan.
He is chairman of Genesis Investment Management Limited and of a number of their closed-end investment funds.
Prior to this he was chairman of Vickers da Costa Limited where he had responsibility for all their Japanese business.
