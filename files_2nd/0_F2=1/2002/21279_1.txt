The Bankers Investment Trust PLC Objectives To maximise shareholders total return by means of a broadly diversified international portfolio.
To achieve long term asset growth in excess of the FTSE All-Share Index.
To achieve regular dividend growth in excess of the increase in the Retail Prices Index.
Capital return excludes all dividend and transaction costs.
