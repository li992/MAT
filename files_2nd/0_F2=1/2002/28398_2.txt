9 Health and safety 9.1 The group attaches great importance to the health and safety of its employees and other people who may be affected by its activities.
9.2 The board has approved a group health and safety policy and a framework for health and safety.
It established the Health and Safety Steering Group which is chaired by executive director Chris Johnstone.
This formally reports to the board each December on health and safety within the group by producing a written report and has a general co-ordination role.
Subsidiary boards are responsible for the issue and implementation of their own health and safety policy as it affects the subsidiary companys day to day responsibility for health and safety.
Each board considers health and safety at a board meeting once a year and produces a written report for the Health and Safety Steering Group.
