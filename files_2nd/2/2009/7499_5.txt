Summary We are pleased to report a year of significant progress for HomeServe and another set of strong results for our membership businesses.
The market opportunities for our three membership businesses are very exciting and we are confident that each of them will continue to deliver strong growth over the coming years.
The decision to exit our UK Emergency Services business will significantly simplify the Group and allow us to focus exclusively on maximising the potential of our membership businesses, both in existing markets and through further international expansion.
Richard Harpin Chief Executive 19 May 2009
