The proposed strategy for the Group over the medium term period was presented by the Executive Directors to the rest of the Board and then debated by the entire Board.
This included a detailed look at the core businesses of the Group, a discussion of new opportunities and areas for expansion, projections for performance over the next financial cycle and a discussion around risks and threats to the Group.
