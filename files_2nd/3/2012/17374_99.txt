The2011 assessment and related report from the external auditors confirmed that the Companys internal control over financial reporting was effective and included in the Companys Annual Report on Form 20-F for the year ended 31 December 2011.
The 2012 assessment and related report will be included in the Companys Annual Report on Form 20-F due to be published in April 2013.
