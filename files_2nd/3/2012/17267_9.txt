Peter Berg Hammer Byg Stark is the market leader for building materials distribution in Denmark.
Having the best people is key to sustaining the number1 market position.
This is achieved through measuring staff engagement onaregular basis and making improvements to working environments and training so that our front line employees areengaged and deliver the best possible service to our customers.
In 2011 12, we achieved a very high average employee engagement score of 76 per cent for all 2,844 Stark employees.
This score was mainly due to more investment in online and on the job training and giving the branch managers more responsibility and autonomy to makedecisions.
The way our employees service our customers is what makes the difference.
This cannot be copied, says Managing Director, Lars Hansen.
