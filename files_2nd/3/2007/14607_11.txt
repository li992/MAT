qxd:13481SPEEDYHI 5 6 07 14:01 Page 42 Report of the Nomination Committee David Wallis, BSc Econ Non-Executive Chairman The terms of reference of the Nomination Committee set out its responsibilities.
These include, inter alia: Reviewing the size and composition of the Board and membership of Board Committees Ensuring that succession planning is in place Making recommendations to the Board on the appointment of Executive and Non-Executive Directors at both Plc and operating level, including job description The reappointment of Directors following their retirement by rotation.
During the course of the year, the Committee met two times.
The Chairmans report on the functioning of the Board on page 20 outlines many of the issues discussed by the Nomination Committee during the course of the year.
Additional and replacement NonExecutive Directors, as well as additional Executive input on the Board, will remain as agenda items during the course of the current financial year.
As the Company has developed an effective People function, there is increasing communication and interaction with the Committee, particularly with regard to succession planning.
In a growing business, it is essential that its rising stars are identified early and given a career path to ensure that they maximize their potential.
This also ensures that the Committee internally has the largest possible pool of talented individuals from which to choose, as well as the option of recruiting externally.
