Each of BHP Billiton Limited and BHP Billiton Plc is a member of the Group, which has its headquarters in Australia.
BHP is a Dual Listed Company structure comprising BHP Billiton Limited and BHP Billiton Plc.
The two entities continue to exist as separate companies but operate as a combined Group known as BHP.
The headquarters of BHP Billiton Limited and the global headquarters of the combined Group are located in Melbourne, Australia.
The headquarters of BHP Billiton Plc are located in London, United Kingdom.
Both companies have identical Boards of Directors and are run by a unified management team.
Throughout this publication, the Boards are referred to collectively as the Board.
Shareholders in each company have equivalent economic and voting rights in the Group as a whole.
In this Annual Report, the terms BHP, Group, BHP Group, we, us, our and ourselves are used to refer to BHP Billiton Limited, BHP Billiton Plc and, except where the context otherwise requires, their respective subsidiaries.
Cross references refer to sections of the Annual Report, unless stated otherwise.
